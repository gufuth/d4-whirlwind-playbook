Reference pages are organized by game system (war plans, cube, charms). This page flips that: pick the OUTCOME, jump to the play. If you want it, do these things in this order.

## I want a mythic seal

The current best path is **Nightmare Escalations → Astaroth** at Torment 10 (NOT 12, drop rates are identical, T10 clears 2x faster per [Sayur's testing](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=F7xU4cMoiAs)).

Backup vector: **Infernal Orbs → Bartuc** with seed-hits loot pool. Add the **Council Coffers** war plan node for a guaranteed 100-ether chest with talisman drops per run.

Skip: Mephisto-only spam. Drop rate is fine but loot density is far worse than Escalations or Orbs.

Cross-reference: [§I. Mythic Seal Loop](/i-mythic-seal-loop/) · [§H. War Plans](/h-war-plans/) (Council Coffers) · [§J. Limited-Time Farms](/j-limited-time-farms/).

## I want a max-roll weapon

The current top-leaderboard tech is **Gem Strength via raw transfig**. Build a 339%+ Gem Strength roll on a weapon and your two grand gems alone provide ~282% damage multi, more than a fully-rolled non-gem-strength weapon.

Workflow:
1. **Farm cheap weapons** to feed the transfig loop. Escalation NMD with **Opal affixes** → loot the opal chest → tens of thousands of obols → gamble obols on weapons.
2. **Raw transfigure each weapon** with Volatile Primordial Dust (from Undercity Tribute of the Harajum bargains).
3. **Lock GAs first** via the enchanter "no change" trick before any cube work.

Cross-reference: [§B. Horadric Cube](/b-horadric-cube/) · [§F. Aspect & Unique Hunting](/f-aspect-unique-hunting/).

## I want gold

The Practice Technique 3-set + Seal of Riches stack delivers ~1,225% gold find with T10's 300% boost. Pair with **Infernal Hordes** runs for billions per session.

## I want materials (gems, dust, etc.)

- **Grand gems**: Sears Reach dungeon boss drops 3 gems per run (royal to grand). [§K's map-marker-on-fringe trick](/k-material-farming/) doubles the run speed.
- **Volatile Primordial Dust** (the transfig fuel): Undercity Tribute of the Harajum bargains, ~12+ per run.
- **Corrupted Roots** (whisper cache farm): drop from existing caches with the Corrupted Roots war plan node, ~20-25% per cache.
