:::tip[Big win]
**Lock GAs via the enchanter no-change trick before any cube work.** Never use Entropic Tuning Prisms on weapons (caps Gem Strength permanently). Kalian Tuning Prism only on amulet (only slot that gets a second Legendary aspect). The mass-transfig pipeline = cheap blue weapons + raw transfig + hunt Gem Strength to 339%+.
:::

Four moves: lock GAs, transfigure weapons raw, Kalian on amulets, right prism for the right roll.

### B1 — Tuning Prism Types

| Prism | What It Does | Use On |
|---|---|---|
| **Entropic** | "Safe" transfig (quality, simple stats). Always makes the item unmodifiable. | Normal armor (helm, gloves, boots, pants, chest) when you can't replace it. **Never weapons.** |
| **Kalian** | Amulet-only. Adds a 2nd Legendary aspect. Item stays modifiable. | Amulet, first transfig pass. |
| **None (raw)** | Random, could brick, could hit Gem Strength jackpot. Can leave item modifiable for a 2nd transfig. | Weapons (always). Items you can replace. |

### B2 — Cube Roll Types

| Roll | Function | Material |
|---|---|---|
| **Add Affix** | Adds a new affix in your chosen category. Also upcycles rarity (white → blue → yellow → legendary). | Coarse + Raw Primordial Dust + tuning prism of category |
| **Focus Reroll** | Rerolls one affix into another in the *same* category. | Tuning prism of that category |
| **Chaotic Reroll** | Rerolls one affix into a *different* category. | Standard reagent |
| **Power Roll** (unique) | Rerolls a unique's special-power percentage. | Tuned Primordial Dust |

### B3 — The "Lock GA" Trick

:::tip[Do]
protocol

The single most important crafting technique. Hard-locks a greater affix against any subsequent cube reroll.

1. Occultist → Enchant tab.
2. Click the GA you want to protect (the star icon).
3. Window shows two re-roll outcomes, don't pick either.
4. Hit "No Change. Materials won't be refunded. Replace."
5. GA loses its star icon but is now hard-locked. The value (e.g., 63% crit damage) stays.
6. Back to the cube. Focus Reroll / Add Affix will never touch the locked GA.

Repeat per GA if you want to lock multiple.

:::

### B4 — Gem Strength: The Endgame Weapon Goal

Transfiguring a weapon raw can roll **Gem Strength**: a multiplier pushing socketed gem damage from ~24% per gem to 105%+. Top leaderboards run 250–333%. Royal/Grand/Horadric/Flawless Horadric gems give larger base bonuses; Gem Strength multiplies whatever the gem provides.

**The chase:** craft cheap blue weapon bases (Blacksmith Forge Equipment, ~330g each) → masterwork → lock GAs → transfigure raw → re-roll. Sometimes a single weapon stays modifiable for a 2nd transfig.

:::tip[Do]
spam method

The mass-transfig pipeline:

pick up every GA weapon you find (don't care about stats). Slam straight into the cube, transfigure raw. Each attempt costs only 1 Primordial Dust. ~30 weapons before one stays modifiable. Once one does: temper, masterwork, enchant, then transfigure a 2nd time for potential double Gem Strength. Same logic applies to mythics (fixed stats, you're hunting the transfig outcome, not the base item).

### B5 — The Amulet Slot

The only slot where Kalian adds a 2nd Legendary aspect AND leaves the item modifiable. The workflow:

| # | Action |
|---|---|
| 1 | Find a 900+ IP amulet with at least 1 GA in a stat you want (crit dmg multi, crit chance, all skills, vuln dmg) |
| 2 | Lock the GA. Add Affix / Focus Reroll to fill out other offensive affixes. |
| 3 | Add a socket. |
| 4 | Imprint your build's most important aspect (e.g., <span class="d4-item" title="Legendary Aspect. While channeling a skill (Whirlwind), all damage is increased by 50-70%x. Always-on for Whirlwind builds.">Aspect of Channeling</span> for <span class="d4-item" title="Core Barbarian skill. Hold-to-channel spin (reworked in Lord of Hatred; no more click-spam). Spawns Dust Devils at level 15+.">Whirlwind</span>). |
| 5 | Transfigure with Kalian → adds a 2nd random Legendary aspect. |
| 6 | Keep Kalian-rerolling until you land on a 2nd aspect you want. |
| 7 | *Optional final step:* a 2nd transfig with Entropic locks the item permanently and adds a small bonus (quality 1–15). Top players sometimes go raw here for higher bonus odds, leaderboard #1 did. |

:::danger[Don't]
bugs

Two acknowledged amulet bugs, patch pending:

- Imprinted aspect doesn't roll at max codex value (e.g., 6.75% instead of 9%).
- After swapping aspects on a Kalian-transfigured amulet, the item can bug out and refuse further transfig.
:::

:::caution[Caveat]
good to know

Sockets stay swappable on unmodifiable items. Even after transfig locks an item, the jeweler can still swap gems. Don't fear gem commitment.
:::

## Gem Strength: the source chain (2026 meta)

The top-leaderboard "secret": weapons with **Gem Strength rolls around 339%** turn each socketed grand gem from 32% into ~141% damage multi. Two gems alone provide ~282% damage multi from the weapon. More than a fully-rolled non-Gem-Strength weapon's total.

How to feed the loop cheaply:

1. **Run Escalation NMD with Opal affixes**. Loot the opal chest at the end.
2. **Convert the obols** at the Purveyor on weapons.
3. **One inventory of gambled weapons** gives a strong chance at one Gem Strength hit per session.
4. **Raw transfigure each weapon** with Volatile Primordial Dust (cheap, ~12 per Undercity Tribute of the Harajum run).
