Warlock launched with Season 13 / Lord of Hatred (April 28, 2026). Both builds use Soul Shard Greater Demons as a class mechanic and share several core items but play very differently.

## Ranking

| # | Build | Best for | Playstyle | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | [Apocalypse](/builds/warlock/apocalypse/) | Push, mythic, all-around | Spam Umbral Chains to stack Apocalypse, then unleash 100-stack nukes | Top of leaderboards. Currently dominating pit pushing |
| 2 | [Profane Sentinel](/builds/warlock/profane-sentinel/) | Speed farm, AoE clear | Summon-driven, you spawn minions and they do the work | Used for AceOfSpades top 10 leaderboard placement |

## What they have in common

- **Heir of Perdition** as the mythic helm of choice (or Crown of Lucion if you don't have it)
- **Footfalls of the Waning World** boots — turns your Evade into Nether Step
- **Nether Step + Metamorphosis + Dark Prison + Blazing Scream** in the core skill bar
- **Mercenary: Raheir + Varyana** reinforcement
- Both want **Critical Strike Chance** capped via Heir + Aspect of Calamity + The Grandfather

## Where they diverge

| Dimension | Apocalypse | Profane Sentinel |
|---|---|---|
| Primary damage | Apocalypse Sigil skill (Hands of the Worldbreaker turns it into a sigil) | Profane Sentinel summons + Blazing Scream recast |
| Stack mechanic | Build to 100 Apocalypse stacks via Hellfire skill hits | Build Shadow Form stacks via Nether Step |
| Mandatory unique | Hands of the Worldbreaker | Footfalls of the Waning World + Seed of Horazon |
| Talisman set | Berú of the Nameless (5pc Sigil set) + Anathema of the Primes | Horizon's Chain (5pc Greater Demon set) + Crown of Lucion charm |
| Soul Shard | Ritualist + Scorching Fragment | Mastermind + Blasphemous Fragment |
| Glyphs | Demonologist + Destruction + Mastermind | Abyssal + Attrition + Demonologist + Mastermind |
| Best for | Pushing, boss kills, screen clears | Speed farming, hands-off AoE |

## Damage diagnosis checklist (Warlock-wide)

1. **Are you crit-capped?** Heir of Perdition + Aspect of Calamity + The Grandfather should put you over 90% crit chance. Below that, Critical Strike Multiplier scaling falls off hard.
2. **Are your gems socketed by slot?** Grand Topaz in amulet, Grand Ruby in rings + weapon, Grand Amethyst in pants + chest.
3. **Are you above the Wrath threshold for resource sustain?** Wrath Regeneration affixes + Unbound glyph + Ring of Starless Skies removes the spam-cap on your core skill.
4. **Are you triggering your talisman set bonuses?** Berú of the Nameless wants Sigil skills active (Apocalypse via Hands of the Worldbreaker counts). Horizon's Chain counts your active Greater Demons.
5. **Did you take the Mythic +1 / -1 Seal bug pair?** If you have both the Mythic -1 Seal and the Mythic 2-Uniques Seal, you can equip 2 unique charms AND the mythic seal at the same time. Confirmed exploit on Mobalytics.
6. **Are you using Scorching Fragment? Then you need Undying Aspect.** Scorching adds Life Cost to your skills; Undying neutralizes it.

## Class mechanic — Soul Shards primer

Warlocks bind to a Soul Shard Greater Demon that fights beside them. Each Shard has on-use abilities + passive effects. Your binding choice changes your build's damage shape more than anything else short of mythic gear.

- **Mastermind Shard** — Grants Summon Laalish skill. Recast skills no longer break Shadowform stealth. Shadowform gives Abyss skills +30%x damage + 5% movement speed per stack. **Profane Sentinel + Blazing Abyss build pick.**
- **Ritualist Shard** — 4% multiplicative damage per Overpower stack. Pair with Scorching Fragment for stackable Volatility damage. **Apocalypse build pick.**
- **Blasphemous Fragment** — Recast skills Hex enemies, +20%x damage from your Abyss Demonology skills.
- **Scorching Fragment** — Volatility stacks to 3, +10%x multiplicative damage per stack. Adds Life Cost (mitigate with Undying Aspect).
- **Mastermind + Blasphemous + Scorching** is the common 3-shard endgame combo for most builds.
