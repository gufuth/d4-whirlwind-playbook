## Damage diagnosis checklist (Warlock-wide)

1. **Are you crit-capped?** <span class="d4-item" title="Mythic Unique Helm. Grants 'Mother's Favor': +60% damage, +20% crit chance, +20% lucky hit, +2 to core skills. Slaying enemies steals favor from allies.">Heir of Perdition</span> + <span class="d4-item" title="Legendary Aspect. Hex increases your critical strike chance. Crit-capping aspect for Warlock.">Aspect of Calamity</span> + <span class="d4-item" title="Mythic Unique 2H Sword. +150%x Critical Strike Damage Multiplier plus large bonuses to all stats.">The Grandfather</span> should put you over 90% crit chance. Below that, Critical Strike Multiplier scaling falls off hard.
2. **Are your gems socketed by slot?** Grand Topaz in amulet, Grand Ruby in rings + weapon, Grand Amethyst in pants + chest.
3. **Are you above the <span class="d4-item" title="Barbarian Glyph. WW-crits historically had a bug with Wrath; pit-test in-game vs Might/Exploit before keeping.">Wrath</span> threshold for resource sustain?** <span class="d4-item" title="Barbarian Glyph. WW-crits historically had a bug with Wrath; pit-test in-game vs Might/Exploit before keeping.">Wrath</span> Regeneration affixes + Unbound glyph + <span class="d4-item" title="Mythic Unique Ring. Massive crit chance + attack speed + lucky hit + Core Skill ranks. Spending resource increases all damage you deal.">Ring of Starless Skies</span> removes the spam-cap on your core skill.
4. **Are you triggering your talisman set bonuses?** <span class="d4-item" title="5-piece Sigil set talisman. Works perfectly with Hands of the Worldbreaker (Apocalypse becomes a Sigil skill).">Berú of the Nameless</span> wants Sigil skills active (<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Ultimate. Converted by Hands of the Worldbreaker into a sigil skill (no cooldown). Stacks to 100 via Hellfire skill hits.">Apocalypse</span> via <span class="d4-item" title="Unique Gloves. Converts your Apocalypse into a Sigil skill (no cooldown). MANDATORY for the Apocalypse Warlock build.">Hands of the Worldbreaker</span> counts). <span class="d4-item" title="5-piece talisman set. Damage scales with active Greater Demons. Profane Sentinel core.">Horizon's Chain</span> counts your active Greater Demons.
5. **Did you take the Mythic +1 / -1 Seal bug pair?** If you have both the Mythic -1 Seal and the Mythic 2-Uniques Seal, you can equip 2 unique charms AND the mythic seal at the same time. Confirmed exploit on Mobalytics.
6. **Are you using <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Soul Shard. Volatility stacks to 3, +10%x multiplicative per stack. Adds Life Cost — mitigate with Undying Aspect.">Scorching Fragment</span>? Then you need <span class="d4-item" title="Legendary Aspect. Mitigates the life cost from Scorching Fragment. Mandatory if running Scorching.">Undying Aspect</span>.** Scorching adds Life Cost to your skills; Undying neutralizes it.

## Class mechanic — Soul Shards primer

Warlocks bind to a Soul Shard Greater Demon that fights beside them. Each Shard has on-use abilities + passive effects. Your binding choice changes your build's damage shape more than anything else short of mythic gear.

- **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Soul Shard. Grants Summon Laalish skill. Recast skills no longer break Shadowform Stealth (costs 2 stacks per cast). Shadowform: +30%x Abyss damage + 5% MS per stack."><span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Glyph. 18%x damage while in Shadowform.">Mastermind</span> Shard</span>**, Grants <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Greater Demon. Bound via Mastermind Shard. Activates talisman set and adds Shadowform stacks.">Summon Laalish</span> skill. Recast skills no longer break Shadowform stealth. Shadowform gives Abyss skills +30%x damage + 5% movement speed per stack. **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock skill. Summons sentinels that apply Vulnerable + AoE damage. Recast adds more sentinels.">Profane Sentinel</span> + Blazing Abyss build pick.**
- **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Soul Shard. 4%x damage per Overpower stack. Apocalypse pick.">Ritualist Shard</span>**, 4% multiplicative damage per Overpower stack. Pair with <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Soul Shard. Volatility stacks to 3, +10%x multiplicative per stack. Adds Life Cost — mitigate with Undying Aspect.">Scorching Fragment</span> for stackable Volatility damage. **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Ultimate. Converted by Hands of the Worldbreaker into a sigil skill (no cooldown). Stacks to 100 via Hellfire skill hits.">Apocalypse</span> build pick.**
- **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Soul Shard. Recast skills Hex enemies on hit, +20%x Abyss Demonology damage.">Blasphemous Fragment</span>**, Recast skills Hex enemies, +20%x damage from your Abyss Demonology skills.
- **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Soul Shard. Volatility stacks to 3, +10%x multiplicative per stack. Adds Life Cost — mitigate with Undying Aspect.">Scorching Fragment</span>**, Volatility stacks to 3, +10%x multiplicative damage per stack. Adds Life Cost (mitigate with <span class="d4-item" title="Legendary Aspect. Mitigates the life cost from Scorching Fragment. Mandatory if running Scorching.">Undying Aspect</span>).
- **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Glyph. 18%x damage while in Shadowform.">Mastermind</span> + Blasphemous + Scorching** is the common 3-shard endgame combo for most builds.
