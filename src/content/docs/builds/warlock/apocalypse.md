## Damage profile

Apocalypse is normally a long-cooldown ultimate. **Hands of the Worldbreaker** (mandatory unique) converts it into a **Sigil skill** with no cooldown. Every Hellfire skill hit generates 1 stack of Apocalypse (up to 100), which scales its damage and size.

The damage chain:

1. **Umbral Chains (Chain Whips rune)** generates persistent hits → builds stacks
2. **Ritualist Shard + Scorching Fragment** Empowers Apocalypse → +50% Additive + Volatility damage
3. **Scorching Fragment** stacks Volatility up to 3 → +10%x Multiplicative per stack
4. **Summon Vollach** overfills your Overpower stacks → +4%x Multiplicative per stack via Ritualist Shard
5. **The Hemat Stone** doubles every Ritualist Shard / Scorching Fragment / Summon Vollach buff → ~300% more damage
6. **Double Blast Apocalypse** node makes Apocalypse deal **double damage** when Empowered

| # | Slot | Item | Why |
|---|---|---|---|

| 2 | **Chest** | Fleshwrit Carapace | Massive damage upgrade — not mandatory but huge over alternatives |
| 3 | **Helm** | Heir of Perdition (mythic) | Best in slot. Attack Speed or Gem Strength transfig. |
| 4 | **Amulet** | The Hemat Stone | Doubles the value of every Ritualist Shard / Scorching Fragment / Summon Vollach buff. Aspect of Rallying Reversal OR Undying Aspect transfig. |
| 5 | **Ring 2** | Ring of Starless Skies (mythic) | +4 All Skills transfig. Removes the spam-cap on Chain Whips Umbral Chains. |
| 6 | **Weapon** | Cremator's Aspect (or The Grandfather for Gem Strength transfig) | Gem Strength on Grandfather > Cremator's. Otherwise Cremator's is better. |
| 7 | **Pants** | Aspect of Heavenly Strength | Enables 2H weapon use for survivability. |
| 8 | **Boots** | Undying Aspect | Counteracts Scorching Fragment's Life Cost. Aspect of Rallying Reversal here if Undying goes on Amulet. |
| 9 | **Ring 1** | Crushing Aspect | Fortify damage scaling |

**Pre-mythic substitutes:** Use a regular amulet with strong rolls instead of forcing Seed of Horazon. Banished Lord's Talisman as charm before mythic.

**Mandatory Soul Shards:**
- **Ritualist Shard** — 4%x damage per Overpower stack
- **Scorching Fragment** — Volatility +10%x per stack up to 3 (life cost mitigated by Undying)

## Skills & rotation

**Skill bar:** Apocalypse · Chain Whips Umbral Chains · Dark Prison · Nether Step · Rampage · Summon Vollach

### Push rotation

1. Stack **Endless Barrage Bombardment** to 5 stacks (procs Fathomless, provides Apocalypse hits)
2. **Nether Step** for DR + mobility
3. **Summon Vollach** to overfill Overpower
4. **Dark Prison** for DR + Fortify
5. **Spam Chain Whips Umbral Chains** (use Blazing Scream instead if bossing)
6. When you hit **80–100 stacks**, cast **Apocalypse**

### Speedfarm rotation

Same as Push but replace Bombardment with **Demonic Smash Rampage** spam for mobility, and use Aspect of Rallying Reversal for the attack speed scaling.

## Paragon, glyphs, talisman

**Mercenary:** Raheir (Ground Slam + Bastion).

**Talisman Seal + 5 Charms: Berú of the Nameless (Sigil set)** — works perfectly because Hands of the Worldbreaker makes Apocalypse a Sigil skill.

**Final unique charm:** **Anathema of the Primes** — lets you use Demonic Smash Rampage as a mobility skill.

:::caution[Mythic Seal Exploit (currently working)]
**A bug can be abused if you have BOTH the Mythic -1 Seal and the Mythic 2-Uniques Seal.** Equip 2 unique charms with the Mythic 2-Uniques Seal, then swap to the Mythic -1 Seal — both effects persist simultaneously. Recommended pairing: Banished Lord's Talisman + Anathema of the Primes.
:::

**Charm affixes priority:**

1. Occult/Hellfire Ranks (Occult is slightly better — also affects Nether Step)
2. All Stats
3. Maximum Life
4. Lightning Resistance
5. Fire Resistance

**Offensive affix priority on gear:**

1. Critical Strike Chance up to 90%
2. Critical Strike Damage Multiplier (strongest once at high crit)
3. Vulnerable Damage Multiplier
4. All Damage / Fire Damage Multiplier
5. Attack Speed
6. Willpower
7. Weapon Damage
8. Maximum Life

**Glyph note:** Apocalypse builds want Demonologist, Destruction, Mastermind. Wrath glyph can swap in for Occultist for more regen.

## What to avoid

:::danger[Don't]
- Don't skip Undying Aspect when running Scorching Fragment. The Life Cost will kill you.
- Don't expect Pyrosis to be better than Fathomless. Pyrosis is only worth more when you're one-shotting; otherwise Fathomless wins (+105%x vs ~20%x in practice).
- Don't use Scepter of the Three — looks attractive (15 Ranks + 200%x multi) but the multi doesn't have enough uptime in practice.
- Don't waste Wrath Regen affixes once you have Ring of Starless Skies. Before Ring of Starless Skies you NEED them.
:::

:::caution[Caveat]
- Build is squishy. Stack Maximum Life almost everywhere you can. Use Cir + Que runes for Barrier generation.
- Use 2H weapon (slightly less damage than 1H + Off-hand) to enable Aspect of Heavenly Strength for survivability.
- Attack Speed has frame breakpoints (server runs at 60fps). Hitting just below a breakpoint wastes all gains above the next breakpoint. Sanctum's stat sheet linked below.
- For more survivability over damage, swap a Banished Lord's Talisman charm for Endurant Faith.
:::
