## Damage profile

<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Ultimate. Converted by Hands of the Worldbreaker into a sigil skill (no cooldown). Stacks to 100 via Hellfire skill hits.">Apocalypse</span> is normally a long-cooldown ultimate. **<span class="d4-item" title="Unique Gloves. Converts your Apocalypse into a Sigil skill (no cooldown). MANDATORY for the Apocalypse Warlock build.">Hands of the Worldbreaker</span>** (mandatory unique) converts it into a **Sigil skill** with no cooldown. Every Hellfire skill hit generates 1 stack of <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Ultimate. Converted by Hands of the Worldbreaker into a sigil skill (no cooldown). Stacks to 100 via Hellfire skill hits.">Apocalypse</span> (up to 100), which scales its damage and size.

The damage chain:

1. **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock skill. Chain Whips rune for persistent hits that build Apocalypse stacks.">Umbral Chains</span> (Chain Whips rune)** generates persistent hits → builds stacks
2. **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Soul Shard. 4%x damage per Overpower stack. Apocalypse pick.">Ritualist Shard</span> + <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Soul Shard. Volatility stacks to 3, +10%x multiplicative per stack. Adds Life Cost — mitigate with Undying Aspect.">Scorching Fragment</span>** Empowers <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Ultimate. Converted by Hands of the Worldbreaker into a sigil skill (no cooldown). Stacks to 100 via Hellfire skill hits.">Apocalypse</span> → +50% Additive + Volatility damage
3. **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Soul Shard. Volatility stacks to 3, +10%x multiplicative per stack. Adds Life Cost — mitigate with Undying Aspect.">Scorching Fragment</span>** stacks Volatility up to 3 → +10%x Multiplicative per stack
4. **Summon Vollach** overfills your Overpower stacks → +4%x Multiplicative per stack via <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Soul Shard. 4%x damage per Overpower stack. Apocalypse pick.">Ritualist Shard</span>
5. **<span class="d4-item" title="Unique Amulet. Doubles the value of every Ritualist Shard / Scorching Fragment / Summon Vollach buff. ~300% more damage scaling for Apocalypse builds.">The Hemat Stone</span>** doubles every <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Soul Shard. 4%x damage per Overpower stack. Apocalypse pick.">Ritualist Shard</span> / <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Soul Shard. Volatility stacks to 3, +10%x multiplicative per stack. Adds Life Cost — mitigate with Undying Aspect.">Scorching Fragment</span> / Summon Vollach buff → ~300% more damage
6. **Double Blast <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Ultimate. Converted by Hands of the Worldbreaker into a sigil skill (no cooldown). Stacks to 100 via Hellfire skill hits.">Apocalypse</span>** node makes <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Ultimate. Converted by Hands of the Worldbreaker into a sigil skill (no cooldown). Stacks to 100 via Hellfire skill hits.">Apocalypse</span> deal **double damage** when Empowered

### Push rotation

1. Stack **Endless Barrage <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock skill. Endless Barrage upgrade stacks to 5 to proc Fathomless. 2. **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Evade replacement (via Footfalls). Generates Shadowform stacks; provides near-immortality during the channel.">Nether Step</span>** for DR + mobility

4. **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock skill. Damage reduction + Fortify + triggers various aspects.">Dark Prison</span>** for DR + Fortify

## What to avoid

:::danger[Don't]
- Don't skip <span class="d4-item" title="Legendary Aspect. Mitigates the life cost from Scorching Fragment. Mandatory if running Scorching.">Undying Aspect</span> when running <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Soul Shard. Volatility stacks to 3, +10%x multiplicative per stack. Adds Life Cost — mitigate with Undying Aspect.">Scorching Fragment</span>. The Life Cost will kill you.
- Don't expect Pyrosis to be better than Fathomless. Pyrosis is only worth more when you're one-shotting; otherwise Fathomless wins (+105%x vs ~20%x in practice).
- Don't use Scepter of the Three, looks attractive (15 Ranks + 200%x multi) but the multi doesn't have enough uptime in practice.
- Don't waste <span class="d4-item" title="Barbarian Glyph. WW-crits historically had a bug with Wrath; pit-test in-game vs Might/Exploit before keeping.">Wrath</span> Regen affixes once you have <span class="d4-item" title="Mythic Unique Ring. Massive crit chance + attack speed + lucky hit + Core Skill ranks. Spending resource increases all damage you deal.">Ring of Starless Skies</span>. Before <span class="d4-item" title="Mythic Unique Ring. Massive crit chance + attack speed + lucky hit + Core Skill ranks. Spending resource increases all damage you deal.">Ring of Starless Skies</span> you NEED them.
:::

:::caution[Caveat]
- Build is squishy. Stack Maximum Life almost everywhere you can. Use <span class="d4-item" title="Rune. Generates 300 offering on 5 skills cast. Overflows Vex to +3 ranks permanently.">Cir</span> + <span class="d4-item" title="Rune. Cooldown-related offering.">Que</span> runes for Barrier generation.
- Use 2H weapon (slightly less damage than 1H + Off-hand) to enable <span class="d4-item" title="Legendary Aspect. Enables 2H weapon use while granting massive damage reduction. Defensive aspect for both Barb and Warlock.">Aspect of Heavenly Strength</span> for survivability.
- Attack Speed has frame breakpoints (server runs at 60fps). Hitting just below a breakpoint wastes all gains above the next breakpoint. Sanctum's stat sheet linked below.
- For more survivability over damage, swap a <span class="d4-item" title="Unique Amulet (also used as charm via Unique Charm). After spending primary resource, your next core skill is guaranteed Overpower. Centerpiece of Overpower builds.">Banished Lord's Talisman</span> charm for <span class="d4-item" title="Unique Charm. Required for the Selig no-life setup. Lets you face-tank Mephisto via massive damage reduction.">Endurant Faith</span>.
:::
