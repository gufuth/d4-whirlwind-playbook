## Strengths and weaknesses

| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|---|---|
| OP AoE | "2 cool 4 school" (per author) |
| Insane speed | Not immune to ground effects |
| High damage | Requires tracking skill durations |
| Nearly immortal during Nether Step uptime | |

## Damage profile

You don't deal the damage directly — **your summons do**. The Horizon's Chain talisman set scales your damage by how many Greater Demons you have active. Stack as many Greater Demons as possible: 5 Profane Sentinels + 3 extra Shard Demons (from the 5-set bonus) + Summon Laalish + Direct Control Blazing Scream + optionally Tyrant's Grasp.

**Damage flow:**

1. **Metamorphosis (Terror Demon)** keeps Shadowform Stealth active → +30%x damage to Abyss skills per stack
2. **Profane Sentinel** applies Vulnerable + deals AoE damage; recast adds more sentinels
3. **Blazing Scream (Direct Control)** counts as a Greater Demon → adds to Horizon's Chain set multiplier
4. **Footfalls of the Waning World** turns Evade into Nether Step + reduces cooldown as Shadowform stacks are consumed
5. **Seed of Horazon** amulet — Demonology Skills deal X%x increased damage per Wrath you have

**Mandatory Soul Shards (per Icy Veins Blazing Abyss):**

- **Mastermind Shard** — Grants Summon Laalish skill. Recast skills no longer break Shadowform Stealth (costs 2 stacks per cast). Shadowform: +30%x Abyss damage + 5% MS per stack.
- **Blasphemous Fragment** — Recast skills Hex enemies, +20%x Abyss Demonology damage.

## Skills & rotation

**Skill bar:** Nether Step · Blazing Scream · Metamorphosis · Dark Prison · Profane Sentinel · Summon Laalish (Greater Demon)

**Rotation (Icy Veins Blazing Abyss order):**

1. **Command Laalish** — adds Shadowform stacks, activates talisman set
2. **Metamorphosis** — puts you in Demonform, massive Shadowform stack pulse, big paragon damage boost, allows Abyss skills without breaking Stealth
3. **Dark Prison** — passive DR + weakens enemies + Fortify
4. **Blazing Scream** — main damage source, duration-based, lets you stay hidden
5. **Profane Sentinel** — second biggest damage source, recast to re-target against tougher enemies (rares, bosses)
6. **Nether Step** — sit in the immortal stealth state with this skill cycling

The trick: cycle Nether Step continuously. You're immortal during the channel. You only pop out for ~0.5 seconds at a time to reapply.

## Paragon, glyphs, talisman

**Mercenary:** Subo (visibility + MS) OR Varyana (Bloodlust attack speed + life leech). Reinforcement: Al'Kin for DR.

**Glyphs:** Abyssal · Attrition · Demonologist · Destruction (with crit) · Mastermind (18%x in Shadowform)

**Talisman Seal: Horizon's Chain (5pc Greater Demon set)** — counts your active Greater Demons. With 5 Profane Sentinels + 3 Shard Demons + Summon Laalish + Direct Control Blazing Scream = ~10 Greater Demons stacked for the set bonus.

**Charm: Crown of Lucion** unique charm — +90%x damage multiplier. Because Blazing Scream is spammable as recast, you can afford to slot Crown of Lucion for the +90%x without resource issues. If running Crown of Lucion as the HELM, use **Rustbitten Dirk** as the charm instead.

## What to avoid

:::danger[Don't]
- Don't drop Metamorphosis. The +Abyss ranks alone make it irreplaceable. Build loses major power without it.
- Don't ignore Direct Control Blazing Scream — its cooldown reduction on Nether Step (via Footfalls) is what makes the spam cycle work.
- Don't try to do the damage yourself. The build is summon-scaled. Stand in the right spot, let demons work.
- Don't put points into Metamorphosis ranks — author notes they don't actually scale anything useful. Free points for elsewhere.
:::

:::caution[Caveat]
- Author plays this on **controller**, says it feels better than keyboard.
- Tyrant's Grasp can be added for more damage at the cost of making the build "a lot more annoying" — only do this for hardcore push runs.
- Pre-Seed of Horazon, a regular high-roll amulet outperforms forcing the mythic.
- This is a Work-in-Progress guide per the author — cross-reference Icy Veins Blazing Abyss (which is more actively maintained) for any conflicts.
:::
