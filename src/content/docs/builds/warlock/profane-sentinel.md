Summon-driven Warlock build. Spawn Profane Sentinels and Greater Demons, sit in near-immortality via <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Evade replacement (via Footfalls). Generates Shadowform stacks; provides near-immortality during the channel.">Nether Step</span> + Shadowform, let your demons clear screens. Used by AceOfSpades for a Season 13 top 10 placement.

## Damage profile

You don't deal the damage directly, **your summons do**. The <span class="d4-item" title="5-piece talisman set. Damage scales with active Greater Demons. Profane Sentinel core.">Horizon's Chain</span> talisman set scales your damage by how many Greater Demons you have active. Stack as many Greater Demons as possible: 5 Profane Sentinels + 3 extra Shard Demons (from the 5-set bonus) + <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Greater Demon. Bound via Mastermind Shard. Activates talisman set and adds Shadowform stacks.">Summon Laalish</span> + <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Blazing Scream passive that makes it count as a Greater Demon for Horizon's Chain set.">Direct Control Blazing Scream</span> + optionally <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock skill. Optional Apocalypse damage add via Abyssal Pit passive. Author note: makes the build 'a lot more annoying' to play.">Tyrant's Grasp</span>.

**Damage flow:**

1. **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Ultimate. Terror Demon form. Mandatory for Profane Sentinel — adds Shadowstep per second and Abyss skill ranks.">Metamorphosis</span> (Terror Demon)** keeps Shadowform Stealth active → +30%x damage to Abyss skills per stack
2. **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock skill. Summons sentinels that apply Vulnerable + AoE damage. Recast adds more sentinels.">Profane Sentinel</span>** applies Vulnerable + deals AoE damage; recast adds more sentinels
3. **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock skill. Main damage source on Profane Sentinel. Duration-based, lets you stay hidden in Shadowform.">Blazing Scream</span> (Direct Control)** counts as a Greater Demon → adds to <span class="d4-item" title="5-piece talisman set. Damage scales with active Greater Demons. Profane Sentinel core.">Horizon's Chain</span> set multiplier
4. **<span class="d4-item" title="Unique Boots. Your Evade becomes Nether Step; Evade cooldown reduced as Shadowform stacks are consumed. Mandatory for the Pelghain frost setup.">Footfalls of the Waning World</span>** turns Evade into <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Evade replacement (via Footfalls). Generates Shadowform stacks; provides near-immortality during the channel.">Nether Step</span> + reduces cooldown as Shadowform stacks are consumed
5. **<span class="d4-item" title="Mythic Unique Amulet. Demonology skills deal increased damage per Wrath stack; you take reduced damage per Dominance stack. Signature Warlock mythic.">Seed of Horazon</span>** amulet, Demonology Skills deal X%x increased damage per <span class="d4-item" title="Barbarian Glyph. WW-crits historically had a bug with Wrath; pit-test in-game vs Might/Exploit before keeping.">Wrath</span> you have

## Skills & rotation

**Skill bar:** <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Evade replacement (via Footfalls). Generates Shadowform stacks; provides near-immortality during the channel.">Nether Step</span> · <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock skill. Main damage source on Profane Sentinel. Duration-based, lets you stay hidden in Shadowform.">Blazing Scream</span> · <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Ultimate. Terror Demon form. Mandatory for Profane Sentinel — adds Shadowstep per second and Abyss skill ranks.">Metamorphosis</span> · <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock skill. Damage reduction + Fortify + triggers various aspects.">Dark Prison</span> · <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock skill. Summons sentinels that apply Vulnerable + AoE damage. Recast adds more sentinels.">Profane Sentinel</span> · <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Greater Demon. Bound via Mastermind Shard. Activates talisman set and adds Shadowform stacks.">Summon Laalish</span> (Greater Demon)

**Rotation (Icy Veins Blazing Abyss order):**

1. **Command Laalish**, adds Shadowform stacks, activates talisman set
2. **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Ultimate. Terror Demon form. Mandatory for Profane Sentinel — adds Shadowstep per second and Abyss skill ranks.">Metamorphosis</span>**, puts you in Demonform, massive Shadowform stack pulse, big paragon damage boost, allows Abyss skills without breaking Stealth
3. **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock skill. Damage reduction + Fortify + triggers various aspects.">Dark Prison</span>**, passive DR + weakens enemies + Fortify
4. **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock skill. Main damage source on Profane Sentinel. Duration-based, lets you stay hidden in Shadowform.">Blazing Scream</span>**, main damage source, duration-based, lets you stay hidden
5. **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock skill. Summons sentinels that apply Vulnerable + AoE damage. Recast adds more sentinels.">Profane Sentinel</span>**, second biggest damage source, recast to re-target against tougher enemies (rares, bosses)
6. **<span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Evade replacement (via Footfalls). Generates Shadowform stacks; provides near-immortality during the channel.">Nether Step</span>**, sit in the immortal stealth state with this skill cycling

The trick: cycle <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Evade replacement (via Footfalls). Generates Shadowform stacks; provides near-immortality during the channel.">Nether Step</span> continuously. You're immortal during the channel. You only pop out for ~0.5 seconds at a time to reapply.

## What to avoid

:::danger[Don't]
- Don't drop <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Ultimate. Terror Demon form. Mandatory for Profane Sentinel — adds Shadowstep per second and Abyss skill ranks.">Metamorphosis</span>. The +Abyss ranks alone make it irreplaceable. Build loses major power without it.
- Don't ignore <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Blazing Scream passive that makes it count as a Greater Demon for Horizon's Chain set.">Direct Control Blazing Scream</span>, its cooldown reduction on <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Evade replacement (via Footfalls). Generates Shadowform stacks; provides near-immortality during the channel.">Nether Step</span> (via Footfalls) is what makes the spam cycle work.
- Don't try to do the damage yourself. The build is summon-scaled. Stand in the right spot, let demons work.
- Don't put points into <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock Ultimate. Terror Demon form. Mandatory for Profane Sentinel — adds Shadowstep per second and Abyss skill ranks.">Metamorphosis</span> ranks, author notes they don't actually scale anything useful. Free points for elsewhere.
:::

:::caution[Caveat]
- Author plays this on **controller**, says it feels better than keyboard.
- <span class="d4-item" title="Warlock skill. Optional Apocalypse damage add via Abyssal Pit passive. Author note: makes the build 'a lot more annoying' to play.">Tyrant's Grasp</span> can be added for more damage at the cost of making the build "a lot more annoying", only do this for hardcore push runs.
- Pre-<span class="d4-item" title="Mythic Unique Amulet. Demonology skills deal increased damage per Wrath stack; you take reduced damage per Dominance stack. Signature Warlock mythic.">Seed of Horazon</span>, a regular high-roll amulet outperforms forcing the mythic.
