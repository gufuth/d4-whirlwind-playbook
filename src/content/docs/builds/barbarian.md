## What they share

All three run **Whirlwind** as the core skill. All three want:

- **Aspect of Channeling** to hold the spin (different slots per variant — see each page)
- **Ramaladni's Magnum Opus** (reworked in Lord of Hatred to apply to ALL skills, not just weapon-tied skills)
- **Gohr's Devastating Grips** for 75–90%x Whirlwind damage + 2s enemy pull. The OLD Gohr's effect is now baseline on the Bursting skill node — both stack.
- **Wrath of the Berserker** for the Heir of Perdition damage window
- **Mercenary:** Raheir (Ground Slam + Bastion + Inspiration). Reinforcement: Varyana.

## Damage diagnosis checklist

If damage feels low on any variant, walk this in order:

1. **Above 50 Primary Resource the whole time?** Aspect of Anger Management's 15–20%x bonus + Berserking only fires above 50 Fury.
2. **Wrath of the Berserker actually up?** Gates the Heir of Perdition damage multiplier window.
3. **Fortified for Crushing Aspect?** Iron Skin generates Fortify on cast. Run it.
4. **Spending Cremator's Aspect windows?** Needs 4+ Overpower stacks. Banished Lord's Talisman charm feeds Overpower on shout cast.
5. **Gems socketed correctly?** Grand Ruby in weapons + armor; Grand Diamond in jewelry.
6. **Took the Bursting node?** Without it you miss the baseline Gohr's-equivalent pulse.
7. **Above 100 Fury for Sescheron's Fury Colossal?** Speed + Selig variants only. 600%x at Colossal is the single biggest talisman-layer multiplier.
8. **Bac+Gar on 2H Bludgeoning, Cir+Vex on 2H Slashing?** Vex overflow gives a permanent +3 to all skills.

Cross-check [§A Damage Foundations](/a-damage-foundations/) for the additive vs multiplicative bucket map. Most damage gaps trace back to stacking same-bucket affixes.

## Known bugs hitting this build family (Patch 3.0.2)

Still live as of May 13, 2026:

:::danger[Limitless Rage passive +Fury/s doesn't stack]
Aspect of Limitless Rage does NOT gain stacks from passive Resource Generation sources. Don't temper for "+X Fury per Second" — use Lucky Hit: Chance to Restore Primary Resource on rings/amulet instead.
:::

:::danger[Crusher Glyph broken on 2H Maces]
The 20% multiplier doesn't fire on 2H Maces. Use **Challenger** as the workaround (most pages here already do).
:::

:::caution[Wrath Glyph + Whirlwind crits — verify in-game]
Longstanding bug report from 2023, no 3.0.2 fix confirmation. Pit-test Wrath socketed vs swapped out for Might or Exploit. If no-Wrath is comparable or faster, swap.
:::
