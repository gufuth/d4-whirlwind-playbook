## What they have in common

Both run **Whirlwind** as the core skill. Both want the same baseline:

- **Aspect of Channeling** weapon for the 50–70%x channeling multiplier
- **Ramaladni's Magnum Opus** (reworked in Lord of Hatred to apply to all skills, not just the weapon's skills, per Icy Veins)
- **Gohr's Devastating Grips** for the 75–90%x Whirlwind damage and 2-second enemy pull (the old Gohrs effect is also now baseline on the Bursting skill node, so both effects stack)
- **Wrath of the Berserker** for the Ugly Bastard / Heir of Perdition damage window
- **Mercenary: Raheir** (Ground Slam + Bastion + Inspiration), reinforcement **Varyana**

## Where they diverge

The d4builds variant 5 leans into **fire conversion** for screen-clearing AoE through Bursting + Cremator's Aspect. The immortal Selig variant trades raw damage for **near-immortality** via Melted Heart of Selig + Overpower scaling — slower clear, but you basically don't die.

## Damage diagnosis checklist

If damage feels low on either build, the same 8-item checklist applies. Walk it in order:

1. **Are you above 50 Primary Resource the whole time?** Aspect of Anger Management's 15–20%x bonus + Berserking only fires above 50 Fury. Fury Generation affixes on chest/pants/boots fix most resource gaps.
2. **Is Wrath of the Berserker actually up?** It's gating the Ugly Bastard / Heir of Perdition fire damage multiplier.
3. **Are you Fortified for Crushing Aspect?** Iron Skin generates Fortify on cast. Run it.
4. **Are you spending Cremator's Aspect windows?** It needs 4+ Overpower stacks. Banished Lord's Talisman charm gives you Overpower on shout cast.
5. **Are gems socketed correctly?** Grand Ruby in weapon + armor for fire damage / strength. Grand Diamond in jewelry.
6. **Did you take the Bursting node?** Without it you miss the baseline Gohr's-equivalent pulse.
7. **Are you above 100 Fury for Sescheron's Fury Colossal stack?** The 5-set gives +50 max Fury and 600%x damage at Colossal. This is the single biggest multiplier in the talisman layer.
8. **Did you put Bac + Gar runes in your 2H bludgeoning and Cir + Vex in your 2H slashing?** The 4-rune setup delivers a permanent +3 to all skills via Vex overflow.

Cross-check against [§A Damage Foundations](/a-damage-foundations/) for the additive vs multiplicative buckets — most damage gaps trace back to stacking same-bucket affixes.
