swa99y's current Pit-push Whirlwind Barb. Selig immortal base layered with three pieces of new tech: **Signet of Pelghain** unique ring, **Thul** frozen-CC rune, and **Azurewrath** for the freeze-expiry explosion payoff. Effectively doubles damage on frozen targets when the rotation is executed correctly.

:::tip[Big win]
**Damage doubles on frozen targets when Azurewrath's explosion fires.** Thul freezes the boss, Whirlwind ticks during the freeze, freeze expires, Azurewrath explodes for 100-120% of all damage you dealt during the freeze window.
:::

## Mercenary

- **Hired:** **Varyana** (no CC, gives ATS instead, which lets you free temper rolls from rings)
- **Reinforcement:** Raheir

Varyana is mandatory for this build because **she applies zero crowd control**. Mercenaries that add CC (Raheir, Subo) break the strict 3-CC discipline that the Pelghain ring depends on.

## How to play with Pelghain — the 3-CC rule

:::tip[Do]
**Three CC sources, never more.**

The build must have exactly three CC sources and no others:

1. Whirlwind via **Gohr's Devastating Grips** (the pull)
2. **Thul** rune frozen
3. **Challenging Shout**
:::

:::danger[Don't]
**No extra control sources.**

- Raheir or Subo as the HIRED merc (they bring CC; Raheir stays as reinforcement only)
- Lucky-hit daze procs on jewelry
- Lucky-hit stun procs on jewelry

More than three CC sources interferes with the Pelghain mechanic.
:::

## How to make Thul your LAST CC on bosses

This is the load-bearing rotation. Get it wrong and Azurewrath's explosion fires off-window or doesn't trigger.

**Thul rune mechanics (verified):**
- Type: Invocation rune
- Cost: **300 Offerings** to fire
- Cooldown: 1 second between fires
- Effect: Frost Nova invoke. Freezes enemies in AOE around you
- Base freeze: 3 seconds. With 80% CCD tempers, caps at **5.4 seconds**

**Bac rune (the Offering feeder):** generates 50 Offerings per 5m traveled. So Thul auto-fires every 30m of movement, which is about 5-6 seconds of moving while channeling Whirlwind.

### The rotation

**Opening (boss spawns):**

1. Whirlwind into the boss and start channeling
2. **Move in tight circles around the boss while channeling.** Whirlwind lets you move during channel. Standing still means no Bac Offerings, which means no Thul, which means no Azurewrath payoff
3. Do NOT fire Challenging Shout yet

**Mid-fight (stagger bar filling):**

4. Your movement accumulates Offerings on Bac. Every ~30m, Thul auto-fires, the boss freezes, the stagger bar ticks up
5. Watch the stagger bar under the boss HP. It rises with each Thul freeze plus each WW Gohr's pull
6. As the stagger bar approaches the top (75-90%), pace your movement so the NEXT Thul firing tips it over 100%

**The trigger moment (stagger about to fill):**

7. Keep moving, keep channeling
8. Thul fires, boss freezes, stagger jumps to 100%. Boss is now **frozen AND staggered simultaneously**
9. **NOW fire Challenging Shout.** CS adds DR and the damage-buff portion, stacking max survivability and damage bonus into the frozen-staggered window

**The damage window (5.4 seconds with max CCD):**

10. Channel the frozen+staggered boss. Don't move much now. You're racking up damage that Azurewrath is tracking
11. Every WW tick, Dust Devil hit, and Overpower proc during this window counts toward Azurewrath's expiry payoff
12. **Freeze expires after 5.4s, Azurewrath fires the explosion** for 100-120% of everything you dealt during the freeze
13. Stagger window may still be running. Keep channeling

**Reset:** return to step 1. Move in circles, build Bac, hold CS for the next cycle.

### Mistakes that break this

**Standing still while channeling.** No Bac = no Thul = no freeze = no Azurewrath payoff. You MUST move during WW.

**Firing CS during build-up.** CS adds a CC chunk to stagger. If you fire it before Thul, CS pushes stagger over the line and Thul lands second. Boss is staggered but not frozen, so no Azurewrath synergy in the stagger window.

**Adding extra CC sources.** Raheir or Subo mercs, lucky-hit daze, lucky-hit stun on jewelry, all of these fill stagger ahead of Thul.

**Spamming WW in tight bursts.** Constant max-channel pulls trigger Gohr's pull repeatedly which fills stagger ahead of Thul. The author's note "don't spam WW" means: be deliberate, channel in measured bursts, let Thul lead.

### How to tell you're doing it right

Visual chain on bosses:

1. WW channeling, you're moving in circles
2. **Blue Frost Nova burst** appears around you (Thul fired)
3. Boss freezes (ice covering)
4. Boss stagger bar **fills at the same instant**
5. The freeze runs ~5 seconds
6. **Bright explosion** from boss when freeze ends (Azurewrath fired)
7. Boss HP drops a giant chunk

If steps 4 and 5 don't coincide, you're firing CS too early or your movement timing is off.

## Author's gear update notes (from planner)

> "Changed exploiter aspect to earthstriker. I discovered that Thul max freeze duration is 5.4 seconds, so we need only 50+ CCD from tempers, but 80% is maximum."

So if you have Exploiter's boots from the old planner snapshot, swap them. Earthstriker's is the current pick.

> "Anger management works partially, works only berserk bonus. So better to have AM aspect on boots, and earthstrikers on selig."

Important nuance: **Anger Management aspect's full effect is bugged.** Only the berserk-bonus portion works. The author's workaround: put **Anger Management aspect on boots**, put **Earthstriker's on Selig (the amulet)** instead of the boots.

## Boss DPS — instant 1-300 fury stacks

- **Resource Generation affixes on rings**
- **Lucky Hit resource tempers on jewelry** (and some weapons)
- **Use Korlic ancient** (the 2-axe ancient). Bigger lucky hit chance than alternatives, removes the need to spam CotA every 6s
- Boss fights still benefit from CotA every 6 sec even with optimization

## Target stats (Pit 145+ comfort push)

- 473 health
- 500+ fury
- 95-100 crit chance
- 91+ ATS
- **50-80% crowd control duration** (Thul caps at 5.4s, so 80% is the ceiling)
- 80k+ armor
- 9-10k+ phys/fire/lightning resist
- 5k+ shadow/cold/poison resist

### Affixes to AVOID on elites

Physical resistance, life link, unstoppable. Damage drops hard against these. Sapper affix is dangerous for survival.

### Wall-corner boss trick

> "Try to kill bosses in wall corners. Don't damage bosses in open area. Try to move it to wall or corner. Dust Devils' winds ricochet from walls and do more damage."
