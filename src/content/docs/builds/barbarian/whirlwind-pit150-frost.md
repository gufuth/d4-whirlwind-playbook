## How it plays

The Pelghain ring is the **gamechanger** that required the rest of the changes:

:::tip[Do]
**Three CC sources, never more.**

The build must have exactly three CC sources and no others:

1. Whirlwind via **Gohr's Devastating Grips** (the pull)
2. **Thul** rune frozen
3. **Challenging Shout**
:::

:::danger[Don't]
**No extra control sources.**

The build deliberately excludes:

- Raheir or Subo as the HIRED merc (they bring CC — Raheir stays as reinforcement only)
- Lucky-hit daze / stun procs

More than three CC sources interferes with the Pelghain mechanic.
:::

### Boss-pull sequence

1. Engage and watch the **staggering bar**
2. When staggering reaches 100%, your last applied CC **must be the Thul rune freeze**
3. **Do not spam Whirlwind during stagger build-up**
4. **Do not cast Challenging Shout before full stagger**
5. Open with everything once Thul is the final CC applied

### Stack ramp on bosses (insta 1–300 stacks)

- **Resource Generation affixes on rings**
- **Lucky Hit resource tempers on jewelry** (and some weapons)
- **Use Korlic ancient** (the guy with 2 axes) — bigger lucky hit chance, removes the need to spam CotA (Call of the Ancients) every 6s
- Boss fights still require a refresh every 6 seconds even with the optimization

## Author's tips

1. **Target stats:** 473 health, 500+ fury, 95–100 crit chance, 91+ ATS, 75k+ armor, 9–10k+ phys/fire/lightning resist, 5k+ shadow/cold/poison resist. (For comfort push at Pit 145+.)
2. **AVOID elite affixes on enemies:** physical resistance, life link, unstoppable. Damage drops hard against these.
3. **Kill bosses in wall corners**, not in open areas. Dust Devils' winds ricochet off walls and deal more damage. Move bosses to corners before engaging.

## How this build differs from [Rob's Spin2Win](/builds/barbarian/whirlwind-speed/)

Both are Selig-based Whirlwind immortals. Shared core: Heir of Perdition + Melted Heart of Selig + Gohr's + Ring of Starless Skies. Different details:

| Slot / decision | swa99y Pelghain Frost | Rob Spin2Win (d4builds v5) |
|---|---|---|
| Ring 2 | **Signet of Pelghain** (frost tech, the upgrade) | Crushing Aspect |
| Chest | Heavenly Strength | Blood Boiling Aspect |
| Pants | Juggernaut's | Aspect of Heavenly Strength |
| Boots | Exploiter's | Aspect of Forward Momentum |
| Runes | Bac · **Thul** · Igni · Ohm | Bac · Jah · Igni · Kel |
| CC strategy | Exactly 3 sources (WW Gohr's + Thul + CS); strict Thul-last-CC boss sequence | Standard rotation, no CC limit |
| Mercenary hired | Varyana (no CC) | Per Rob's setup (different) |
| Talisman seal | Legendary Horadric Seal + 5 Crucible charms + Banished Lord's + Ramaladni's | Per Rob's setup |

The Pelghain ring + frost tech is the meaningful difference — meant to push higher Pit levels via the Thul stagger sequence. If you want to try it, the upgrade path from Rob's Spin2Win is essentially: swap Ring 2 → Signet of Pelghain, swap Jah → Thul rune, restrict CC sources to 3, change merc to Varyana.
