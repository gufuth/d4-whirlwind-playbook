## Damage profile

Overpower-scaled, NOT Selig no-life. Damage flows through three multiplicative buckets that don't compete (see [§A. Damage Foundations](/a-damage-foundations/)):

- **Channel + Berserk bucket**, Aspect of Channeling (amulet) for 50%x while channeling, Heir of Perdition for 80%x permanent, Wrath of the Berserker for Berserking
- **Overpower bucket**, Heavy Hitting Aspect (ring) for tornado damage, Blood Boiling synergy via Tibault's Will + Dominate Glyph
- **Crit bucket**, Ring of Starless Skies + Grandfather + Critical Strike Damage Multiplier on rings/amulet

Heir of Perdition's Mother's Favor (+60% damage, +20% crit chance, +20% lucky hit, +2 to core skills) plus Ring of Starless Skies takes massive pressure off your other gear for baseline crit. That frees temper and masterwork rolls on every other slot for Whirlwind damage and physical multipliers.

## Known bugs that hit this build (Patch 3.0.2)

Both still live as of May 13, 2026:

:::danger[Don't waste rolls on passive Fury per Second]
**Aspect of Limitless Rage does NOT gain stacks from passive Resource Generation sources.** Any "+X Fury per Second" affix on helm or other slots is wasted for stack building. The aspect still works through Fury spent on Whirlwind itself + active resource generators (Rallying Cry, Shouts), so it's still mandatory, but don't chase passive fury-per-second rolls.
:::

:::danger[Don't socket Crusher on a 2H Mace]
**Crusher Glyph's 20% multiplier doesn't work with 2H Maces.** You're already running Challenger as the workaround, keep it. If you ever swap to a non-Mace 2H, Crusher returns to viability.
:::

:::caution[Verify Wrath in-game]
**Longstanding "Wrath does not work with Whirlwind crits" bug**, last confirmed-broken report from 2023, no Patch 3.0.2 fix confirmation. Pit-test it with Wrath socketed vs. swapped out for Might or Exploit. If the no-Wrath run is comparable or faster, swap. A dead glyph slot is the most expensive form of waste at endgame.
:::

## Top upgrade lanes (impact-ordered)

### 1. Amulet Kalian transfiguration

**Single largest upgrade available.** The amulet is the only slot that can carry **two** Legendary aspects (via Kalian Tuning Prism). You currently have one (Aspect of Channeling). Adding a second offensive aspect (Edgemaster, Dire Whirlwind) or a strong defensive aspect doubles the slot's value.

Process: fully masterwork → Kalian transfigure → reroll until you land a strong second aspect → lock with Standard Reagent. See [§B. Horadric Cube](/b-horadric-cube/) for the Lock GA trick discipline.

### 2. Verify glyph #5 in-game (Wrath)

Pit 80 with Wrath socketed. Same Pit with Wrath removed and Might or Exploit substituted. If no-Wrath is comparable or faster, swap. High-value because the cost of a broken glyph is huge at endgame.

### 3. Rebuild any +Fury per Second temper rolls

Limitless Rage's passive-resource bug makes them dead weight. Replace with:
- Rings/amulet: Lucky Hit: Chance to Restore Primary Resource
- Other slots: % Whirlwind damage, crit chance

### 4. Talisman audit

You haven't named your current Talisman + Charm setup. Banished Lord's Talisman is the gold-standard centerpiece for Overpower Whirlwind. Drop a Mythic Seal + 5 charms in your current loadout and I can do a slot-by-slot pass.

### 5. Farm El'druin (Tyrael's Sword of Justice)

New Lord of Hatred mythic 1H sword. Per Mobalytics, sits at #2 on the mythic priority for Overpower Whirlwind, ahead of Grandfather. **Effect:** defeating an Elite pack reduces all skill cooldowns by 10s for 12s. This turbocharges your Rallying Cry / War Cry uptime (the engine that actually generates Limitless Rage stacks since passive Fury/sec is bug-disabled).

Farm via: Lair Bosses (especially Exalted), the Kurast Undercity tribute system, Helltides, and Purveyor of Curiosities. Slot it as a 1H Arsenal weapon (replacing the Crushing Aspect 1H).

### 6. Tyrael's Might vs. Blood Boiling chest tradeoff

Decision, not a recommendation. The inputs are your Pit ceiling and how often you die:

- **Comfortably clearing T11-T12 with Tyrael's:** Keep Tyrael's.
- **Getting one-shot or struggling on bosses:** Chest isn't the problem; look at paragon defensive nodes, glyph 50 affixes, masterwork resists.
- **Cruising on survival and want to push damage hard:** A Legendary chest with **Blood Boiling Aspect** (grants +1 Overpower stack every ~4 seconds, with Dominate Glyph synergy keeping you at 4 stacks 100% of the time) plus masterworked defensive affixes will out-damage Tyrael's Might at the cost of meaningfully less raw toughness.

## Skill bar + rotation

**Skill bar:** Whirlwind · Iron Skin · Wrath of the Berserker · Rallying Cry · War Cry · Challenging Shout

1. **Rallying Cry**, maintain. Costs Fury but ~60% generation boost outpaces the cost
2. **Whirlwind** through enemies, channels Aspect of Channeling permanently
3. **Wrath of the Berserker** on cooldown, gates Heir of Perdition's window + makes you unstoppable
4. **War Cry** for burst against elites/bosses
5. **Challenging Shout** when health drops
6. **Iron Skin** when you need Fortify quickly
