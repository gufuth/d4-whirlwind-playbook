## Damage profile

Overpower-scaled, NOT Selig no-life. Damage flows through three multiplicative buckets that don't compete (see [§A. Damage Foundations](/a-damage-foundations/)):

- **Channel + Berserk bucket**, <span class="d4-item" title="Legendary Aspect. While channeling a skill (Whirlwind), all damage is increased by 50-70%x. Always-on for Whirlwind builds.">Aspect of Channeling</span> (amulet) for 50%x while channeling, <span class="d4-item" title="Mythic Unique Helm. Grants 'Mother's Favor': +60% damage, +20% crit chance, +20% lucky hit, +2 to core skills. Slaying enemies steals favor from allies.">Heir of Perdition</span> for 80%x permanent, <span class="d4-item" title="Barbarian Ultimate. Enter Berserking; gain Unstoppable and large damage bonus. Gates the Heir of Perdition / Ugly Bastard damage window.">Wrath of the Berserker</span> for Berserking
- **Overpower bucket**, <span class="d4-item" title="Legendary Aspect. Amplifies Tornado damage via the Whirlwind Tornado choice node. Strong for Overpower variants.">Heavy Hitting Aspect</span> (ring) for tornado damage, Blood Boiling synergy via <span class="d4-item" title="Unique Pants. Massive resource generator. Generates 200% increased resource while Unstoppable. Lets Whirlwind builds drop most regen sources.">Tibault's Will</span> + <span class="d4-item" title="Barbarian Glyph. Damage scales with Overpower charges. Maintains 4 stacks 100% of the time with Blood Boiling.">Dominate</span> Glyph
- **Crit bucket**, <span class="d4-item" title="Mythic Unique Ring. Massive crit chance + attack speed + lucky hit + Core Skill ranks. Spending resource increases all damage you deal.">Ring of Starless Skies</span> + Grandfather + Critical Strike Damage Multiplier on rings/amulet

Slaying enemies steals favor from allies.">Heir of Perdition</span>'s Mother's Favor (+60% damage, +20% crit chance, +20% lucky hit, +2 to core skills) plus <span class="d4-item" title="Mythic Unique Ring. ## Top upgrade lanes (impact-ordered)

### 1. Amulet Kalian transfiguration

**Single largest upgrade available.** The amulet is the only slot that can carry **two** Legendary aspects (via Kalian Tuning Prism). You currently have one (<span class="d4-item" title="Legendary Aspect. While channeling a skill (Whirlwind), all damage is increased by 50-70%x. Always-on for Whirlwind builds.">Aspect of Channeling</span>). Adding a second offensive aspect (Edgemaster, Dire <span class="d4-item" title="Core Barbarian skill. Hold-to-channel spin (reworked in Lord of Hatred; no more click-spam). Spawns Dust Devils at level 15+.">Whirlwind</span>) or a strong defensive aspect doubles the slot's value.

Process: fully masterwork → Kalian transfigure → reroll until you land a strong second aspect → lock with Standard Reagent. See [§B. Horadric Cube](/b-horadric-cube/) for the Lock GA trick discipline.

### 2. Verify glyph #5 in-game (Wrath)

Pit 80 with <span class="d4-item" title="Barbarian Glyph. WW-crits historically had a bug with Wrath; pit-test in-game vs Might/Exploit before keeping.">Wrath</span> socketed. Same Pit with <span class="d4-item" title="Barbarian Glyph. WW-crits historically had a bug with Wrath; pit-test in-game vs Might/Exploit before keeping.">Wrath</span> removed and <span class="d4-item" title="Barbarian Glyph. Defensive + damage. Standard Speed-variant pick.">Might</span> or <span class="d4-item" title="Barbarian Glyph. Vulnerable damage scaling. Standard pick for Push and Speed variants.">Exploit</span> substituted. If no-<span class="d4-item" title="Barbarian Glyph. WW-crits historically had a bug with Wrath; pit-test in-game vs Might/Exploit before keeping.">Wrath</span> is comparable or faster, swap. High-value because the cost of a broken glyph is huge at endgame.

### 3. Rebuild any +Fury per Second temper rolls

Limitless Rage's passive-resource bug makes them dead weight. Replace with:
- Rings/amulet: Lucky Hit: Chance to Restore Primary Resource
- Other slots: % <span class="d4-item" title="Core Barbarian skill. Hold-to-channel spin (reworked in Lord of Hatred; no more click-spam). Spawns Dust Devils at level 15+.">Whirlwind</span> damage, crit chance

### 4. Talisman audit

You haven't named your current Talisman + Charm setup. <span class="d4-item" title="Unique Amulet (also used as charm via Unique Charm). After spending primary resource, your next core skill is guaranteed Overpower. Drop a Mythic Seal + 5 charms in your current loadout and I can do a slot-by-slot pass.

### 5. Farm El'druin (Tyrael's Sword of Justice)

New Lord of Hatred mythic 1H sword. Per Mobalytics, sits at #2 on the mythic priority for Overpower <span class="d4-item" title="Core Barbarian skill. Hold-to-channel spin (reworked in Lord of Hatred; no more click-spam). Spawns Dust Devils at level 15+.">Whirlwind</span>, ahead of Grandfather. **Effect:** defeating an Elite pack reduces all skill cooldowns by 10s for 12s. This turbocharges your <span class="d4-item" title="Barbarian shout. Massive resource generation buff. WARNING: do NOT spam — only refresh when buff drops, or you waste Fury Ramaladni's needs.">Rallying Cry</span> / <span class="d4-item" title="Barbarian shout. Damage buff vs elites and bosses.">War Cry</span> uptime (the engine that actually generates Limitless Rage stacks since passive Fury/sec is bug-disabled).

Farm via: Lair Bosses (especially Exalted), the Kurast Undercity tribute system, Helltides, and Purveyor of Curiosities. Slot it as a 1H Arsenal weapon (replacing the <span class="d4-item" title="Legendary Aspect. While Fortified, you deal 45-65%x increased damage. Iron Skin uptime keeps this on.">Crushing Aspect</span> 1H).

### 6. Tyrael's Might vs. Blood Boiling chest tradeoff

Decision, not a recommendation. The inputs are your Pit ceiling and how often you die:

- **Comfortably clearing T11-T12 with Tyrael's:** Keep Tyrael's.
- **Getting one-shot or struggling on bosses:** Chest isn't the problem; look at paragon defensive nodes, glyph 50 affixes, masterwork resists.
- **Cruising on survival and want to push damage hard:** A Legendary chest with **<span class="d4-item" title="Legendary Aspect. Grants 1 Overpower charge every ~4 seconds. With Dominate Glyph, maintains 4 stacks ~100% of the time. Most important aspect of the Overpower Whirlwind variant.">Blood Boiling Aspect</span>** (grants +1 Overpower stack every ~4 seconds, with <span class="d4-item" title="Barbarian Glyph. Damage scales with Overpower charges. Maintains 4 stacks 100% of the time with Blood Boiling.">Dominate</span> Glyph synergy keeping you at 4 stacks 100% of the time) plus masterworked defensive affixes will out-damage <span class="d4-item" title="Mythic Unique Chest. Defensive backbone (huge toughness + resists) with All Damage and Maximum Resistance affixes.">Tyrael's Might</span> at the cost of meaningfully less raw toughness.
