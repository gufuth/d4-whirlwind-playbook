## Damage profile

Damage is **Overpower-driven**, not raw Fury-spend. Overpower fires guaranteed crits scaled to Fortify total, which is amplified by every Fortify-boosting affix and aspect. The build's identity isn't "spin fast", it's "spin while immortal long enough to win every fight."

Three contributing buckets:

- **Overpower bucket**, Overpower Damage Multiplier affixes on rings + amulet, plus <span class="d4-item" title="Legendary Aspect. Slain Burning enemies have 10% chance to grant Overpower. Fire damage increased by 15-20%x.">Overheating Aspect</span> Overpower chance on slain Burning enemies
- **Fortify bucket**, <span class="d4-item" title="Legendary Aspect. While Fortified, you deal 45-65%x increased damage. Iron Skin uptime keeps this on.">Crushing Aspect</span> (while Fortified) + <span class="d4-item" title="Barbarian defensive. Generates Fortify on cast; activates Tibault's Will Unstoppable proc.">Iron Skin</span> uptime + Fortify generation affixes
- **Channel + Berserk bucket**, same <span class="d4-item" title="Legendary Aspect. While channeling a skill (Whirlwind), all damage is increased by 50-70%x. Always-on for Whirlwind builds.">Aspect of Channeling</span> + <span class="d4-item" title="Mythic Unique Helm. Grants 'Mother's Favor': +60% damage, +20% crit chance, +20% lucky hit, +2 to core skills. Slaying enemies steals favor from allies.">Heir of Perdition</span> as the meta variant

## What to avoid

:::danger[Don't]
- Don't stack Maximum Life affixes, they're wasted slots in the no-life build. Use those slots for Fury Generation, damage multipliers, or resistances instead.
- Don't drop below ~100 Fury sustained. That's the <span class="d4-item" title="5-piece talisman set. 2-set: Colossal at 100+ Fury (20% size, 120%x damage, -50% cast speed). 3-set: +10 Fury when hit, +30% Max Life. 5-set: +50 Max Fury, +50% size, 600%x damage at Colossal.">Sescheron's Fury</span> Colossal threshold AND your Selig damage runway. Both vanish together if you go below.
- Don't use Selig before Patch 3.0.2 buffs landed if you're reading this guide later, the build assumes current patch behavior.
- Don't ignore resistances. Selig converts damage to resource at a 1:1-ish ratio; reducing the damage taken in the first place stretches Fury further.
:::
