## Damage profile

The build converts Whirlwind damage to **fire** via the Bursting choice node and the Ugly Bastard Helm conditional (or Heir of Perdition if you have it). Damage flows through three multiplicative buckets that don't compete:

- **Channeling bucket** — Aspect of Channeling weapon gives 50–70%x while channeling. Whirlwind is a continuous channel, so this is always live.
- **Berserking + fire bucket** — Ugly Bastard / Heir of Perdition during Wrath windows + Overheating Aspect from slain Burning enemies.
- **Crit bucket** — Crit Chance affixes on amulet/rings + Crit Damage Multiplier on gloves/amulet + Edgemaster's Aspect scaling on amulet.

The Bursting node fires the old Gohr's-equivalent pulse for free, so the Gohrs Devastating Grips unique stacks ON TOP of that with its own 75–90%x bonus + 2-second enemy pull. Stacking those two is the single biggest reason this variant beats older Whirlwind metas.

**Gems:** Grand Ruby in weapons (fire damage), Grand Ruby in armor (strength), Grand Diamond in jewelry (resistances).

**Runewords (2 max, two-handed weapons):**
- 2H Bludgeoning: **Bac + Gar** → Bac generates 50 Offering per 5m traveled; Gar consumes 25 Offering for stacking 2.5% Crit Chance up to 10%
- 2H Slashing: **Cir + Vex** → Cir generates 300 Offering on 5 skills cast; Vex consumes 100 Offering for +1 all skills, overflows to +3 ranks for 10 seconds. Combined cooldowns mean you get a permanent +3 to all skills.

## Skills & rotation

**Skill bar:** Whirlwind · Iron Skin · Wrath of the Berserker · Rallying Cry · War Cry · Challenging Shout

**Rotation:**

1. **Rallying Cry** — Maintain for resource (costs some Fury but ~60% generation boost outpaces the cost)
2. **Whirlwind** through enemies — burns them via Bursting node, channels Aspect of Channeling and Edgemaster's
3. **Wrath of the Berserker** on cooldown — gates Ugly Bastard / Heir of Perdition + makes you unstoppable
4. **War Cry** for burst windows against elites/bosses
5. **Challenging Shout** when health is dropping
6. **Iron Skin** when you need fortify quickly or as the death-prevention button

## Paragon, glyphs, talisman

**Mercenary:** Raheir the Shieldbearer (Ground Slam + Raheir's Aegis + Bastion + Inspiration). Reinforcement: **Varyana** with War Cry opportunity + Bloodthirst.

**Talisman Seal:** Legendary seal with +1 Charm Slot. Other affixes: damage while berserking, damage reduction, resistances, life.

**Charm set: Sescheron's Fury (5 piece)** — the single biggest damage multiplier in the talisman layer:
- 2-set: Colossal at 100+ Fury → 20% character + skill size, 120%x damage, but 50% cast speed loss
- 3-set: +10 Fury when hit, +30% Maximum Life
- 5-set: +50 Max Fury, Colossal further increases size by 50% and damage to **600%x**

**Final charm:** Unique Charm with **Banished Lord's Talisman** power → easy Overpower stacks for Cremator's Aspect + bonus Crit Multiplier.

Glyphs at level 50 (radius) then 51 (secondary multiplier). Some paragon points locked behind Season Rank.

## What to avoid

:::danger[Don't]
- Don't use Entropic Tuning Prisms on weapons. Caps Gem Strength permanently below the meta. (See [§B Horadric Cube](/b-horadric-cube/).)
- Don't skip Bac+Gar / Cir+Vex runeword setup. The permanent +3 all-skills from Vex overflow is huge.
- Don't run without Iron Skin in the bar. You lose Fortified uptime, which kills Crushing Aspect's 45–65%x.
- Don't forget Wrath of the Berserker on cooldown — it gates the helm's biggest multiplier.
- Don't waste a slot on Tidal Aspect once you have Ring of Starless Skies. Drop Tidal immediately for the mythic.
:::

:::caution[Caveat]
- Sescheron's Fury Colossal costs 50% cast speed for the 600%x damage. Pre-cast shouts BEFORE going Colossal so you don't fight your buttons.
- The build has lower single-target damage than some alternatives. For pure bossing, drop a charm slot for a single-target multiplier or consider a different build for boss-specific runs.
- Aspect of Anger Management drains 3 Primary Resource Regeneration — you need Fury Generation affixes on chest/pants/boots to keep up.
:::
