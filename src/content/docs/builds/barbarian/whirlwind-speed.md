## What this build is

Speed immortal. Built around staying spinning continuously while taking near-zero damage. Optimized for clear rate over single-target nuke. Aspect of Channeling holds the spin permanently; immortality comes from layered defensive aspects rather than the Melted Heart of Selig no-life trick. Distinct from your current [Overpower main](/builds/barbarian/whirlwind-overpower/): this one trades Tyrael's Might + Overpower scaling for movement and survivability.

## Damage diagnosis — why yours feels light

Walk this checklist. The Speed variant is more sensitive than the Overpower variant to a few specific things because it doesn't have Tyrael's Might + Overpower scaling masking gaps:

1. **Aspect of Channeling on a 2H weapon, not amulet.** Speed wants Channeling on the 2H slot for the 100% potency bonus. Your Overpower gearset has it on the amulet for 50% potency — correct for Overpower, but underweights Channeling in Speed.
2. **Sescheron's Fury 5-set talisman.** Speed wants Colossal's 600%x damage multiplier above 100 Fury. If your talisman is still on Banished Lord's (correct for Overpower), Speed suffers.
3. **Chest swap.** Speed runs a Legendary chest with Aspect of Disobedience or Blood Boiling. Your Overpower spec uses Tyrael's Might in chest — correct for Overpower, wrong for Speed. Speed already has immortality from Channeling + Iron Skin uptime; the chest slot is for damage multipliers.
4. **Glyph swap.** Speed uses Dominate / Twister / Challenger / **Might** / **Exploit**. Your Overpower glyphs are Dominate / Twister / Challenger / Marshal / Wrath. Swap Marshal → Might and Wrath → Exploit for Speed runs.

In short: **Speed needs a separate gearset.** Don't expect speed numbers when running Speed on Overpower gear — you pay both the speed-optimization tax and the gear-mismatch tax.

## Skills & rotation

**Skill bar:** Whirlwind · Iron Skin · Wrath of the Berserker · Rallying Cry · War Cry · Challenging Shout

1. **Rallying Cry** — maintain for resource generation
2. **Whirlwind** through enemies, burns via Bursting node
3. **Wrath of the Berserker** on cooldown — gates the Heir of Perdition damage window
4. **War Cry** + **Iron Skin** on cooldown for damage + survivability layers
5. **Challenging Shout** when health is dropping

## Paragon, glyphs, talisman

**Mercenary:** Raheir (Ground Slam + Bastion + Inspiration). Reinforcement: **Varyana** (War Cry opportunity + Bloodthirst).

**Talisman: Sescheron's Fury (5pc set).** The 5-set's 600%x Colossal damage multiplier at 100+ Fury is the single biggest damage scaler in the Speed variant.

**Charm: Banished Lord's Talisman** unique power for Overpower stacks — feeds Cremator's Aspect.

**Glyphs (level 50 then 51 for the secondary multiplier):** Dominate · Twister · Challenger · **Might** · **Exploit**. Swaps Marshal and Wrath out vs your Overpower lineup.

## What to avoid

:::danger[Don't]
- Don't run Speed on your Overpower gear. The chest, talisman, glyphs, and amulet aspect all differ. Use a separate gearset.
- Don't keep Aspect of Channeling on the amulet for Speed. Move it to the 2H Bludgeoning weapon.
- Don't socket Marshal or Wrath for Speed runs. Use Might and Exploit.
- Don't skip Bac+Gar / Cir+Vex runeword pairs on the two-handers.
:::

:::caution[Caveat]
- Single-target damage is lower than your Overpower variant by design. For boss-only runs, swap back to Overpower.
- Sescheron's Fury Colossal costs 50% cast speed in exchange for the 600%x. Pre-cast shouts before going Colossal so you don't fight your buttons.
- The original guide intends this for "any game, any content" — pit pushing comes second to clear rate.
:::
