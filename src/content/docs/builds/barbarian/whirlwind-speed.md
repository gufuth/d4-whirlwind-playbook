## How to keep up Fury (verbatim from Rob's notes)

- Fury Regeneration on **at least 2 pieces** (Chest / Pants / Boots)
- **Fury Generation Temper on Rings**
- Use **Rallying Cry ONLY when the buff runs out**, DO NOT SPAM IT

## Rotation (verbatim)

1. Keep up **Challenging Shout · Iron Skin · War Cry** continuously
2. **Rallying Cry** has no cooldown now but costs Fury. Spam only to keep the Fury buff up.
3. Use **Wrath of the Berserker** vs Elites & Bosses
4. Spin with **Whirlwind** and start winning, *you can now HOLD it down* (continuous channel, no spam-click)

## How to become immortal — breakpoints (verbatim)

- **534 Life** (NOT MORE THAN 600)
- **400+ Fury** (roll Charms to Fury)
- **30+ Fury Generation** (roll Fury Gen on Boots + Pants + Jewelry)
- Lots of **Damage Reduction %**
- **Melted Heart of Selig** (also reduces life, required for the no-life mechanic)
- **Endurant Faith** Unique Charm

## Why your damage might be light

Without seeing your specific roll states, common diagnostic checklist (cross-reference [§A. Damage Foundations](/a-damage-foundations/) for the multiplicative bucket map):

1. **Are you at the immortal breakpoints?** 534 Life, 400+ Fury, 30+ Fury Generation. Missing any one of these knocks the whole loop out of sustain.
2. **Endurant Faith charm equipped?** It's a hard requirement Rob lists, without it the Selig no-life loop doesn't survive boss hits.
3. **Are you spamming Rallying Cry?** Rob's note is explicit, DO NOT SPAM. Only when the buff drops. Over-casting eats Fury you need for Whirlwind.
4. **Holding Whirlwind down, not click-spamming?** The Lord of Hatred Whirlwind rework made it a true hold-to-channel. Old click-spam habits leak DPS.
5. **Wrath of the Berserker on cooldown vs elites/bosses?** Gates the Heir of Perdition damage window.
6. **Bac+Jah / Igni+Kel runes active?** Rob's runeword setup is specific, substituting different runes likely costs damage.

## What this build is NOT

- **Not** your current [Overpower Whirlwind](/builds/barbarian/whirlwind-overpower/), that one runs Tyrael's Might in chest + Heavy Hitting ring + Wrath/Marshal glyphs, scales through Overpower stacks. This is a Selig-based immortal, completely different damage scaling.
- **Not** the [swa99y Pelghain Frost variant](/builds/barbarian/whirlwind-pit150-frost/), closely related (also Selig-based, mostly same items) but that one adds the new **Signet of Pelghain** unique ring + **Thul** rune for frost CC. Different rotation rules apply (3 CC sources max, Thul-last-CC boss stagger sequence). The Pelghain variant is the upgrade path from this build if you want to try the new tech.
