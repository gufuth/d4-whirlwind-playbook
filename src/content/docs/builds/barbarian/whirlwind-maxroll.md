## Overpower swap (after Torment 12 + Dominate Glyph Lv. 100 + Banished Lord's Talisman)

Three swap directions documented. All require you to swap into <span class="d4-item" title="Unique Amulet (also used as charm via Unique Charm). After spending primary resource, your next core skill is guaranteed Overpower. Centerpiece of Overpower builds.">Banished Lord's Talisman</span> as the centerpiece charm and level <span class="d4-item" title="Barbarian Glyph. Damage scales with Overpower charges. Maintains 4 stacks 100% of the time with Blood Boiling.">Dominate</span> to 100.

### Speedfarm Overpower

- Best for general play
- High damage output
- Incredible speed farmer AND boss farmer
- Easily farms **Pit 125**
- Kills last Mephisto phase in 2-3 seconds
- Easy to swap into
- Decently tanky

### Push Overpower

- Very tanky
- Highest damage output
- Incredible Tower pusher
- Tower 140+ with good gear
- **Tower 150+ with amazing Transfigured gear**
- Easy to swap into
- Requires proper cooldown management to maintain Fury

### Push Selig Overpower

- Exceptionally tanky
- Strong Tower pusher
- Tower 135+ with good gear
- Tower 145+ with amazing Transfigured gear
- Harder to swap into
- **Requires a whole new gearset with absolutely no life**
- Can face-tank Mephisto with **<span class="d4-item" title="Unique Charm. Required for the Selig no-life setup. Lets you face-tank Mephisto via massive damage reduction.">Endurant Faith</span>** charm

(The Selig Overpower variant is the same family as [Rob Spin2Win](/builds/barbarian/whirlwind-speed/) and [swa99y Pelghain Frost](/builds/barbarian/whirlwind-pit150-frost/). They differ in talisman, ring choices, and CC strategy.)
