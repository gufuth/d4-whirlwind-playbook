The Maxroll endgame Whirlwind Barbarian guide. Hosted on wudijo's content creator profile, actually authored by **AoinoMiku**. Six configurations documented: Starter, Midgame, Endgame default, and three Overpower swaps (Speedfarm, Push, Push Selig).

Distinct from the two builds you already have tracked:

- This page is the **Maxroll-meta default** (Tibault's Will + Channeling amulet + Sescheron's Fury 5-set + Crown of Lucion charm). No Pelghain frost tech, no Tyrael's Might chest.
- [Rob Spin2Win](/builds/barbarian/whirlwind-speed/) is the d4builds variant 5 (different chest, runes, mercenary).

## Equipment notes (verbatim from guide)

- **Gohr's Devastating Grips** is the core of any Whirlwind build. Get it ASAP, including upgrading common gloves in the cube to gamble for it.
- **Tibault's Will is mandatory** for the Midgame swap. Insane resource generator that lets you drop other regen sources AND comfortably run Crown of Lucion.
- Prioritize **Aspect of Anger Management** on top of Offensive Aspects (gamble Obols on Gloves, upgrade to Legendary in the cube).
- **Ring of Starless Skies** is optional for smoother Fury management, but a high-roll Legendary ring provides slightly more damage. Not required.

## Skills (default Endgame)

83 points spent. Skill bar centers Whirlwind + the shout package.

Rotation (verbatim):

1. **Always be spinning** and moving (in circles or in lines) with **Whirlwind**.
2. Use **War Cry**, **Challenging Shout**, and **Call of the Ancients** to buff damage on tougher enemies.
3. **Highlander upgrade on Call of the Ancients** is core. Reduces CotA cooldown to 2 seconds for constant uptime on Attack Speed + Damage Bonus.
4. **Iron Skin on cooldown** to proc Tibault's Will Unstoppable.
5. **Rallying Cry** when the previous buff is about to run out. **Don't spam it.** Running out of Fury reduces Ramaladni's Magnum Opus effectiveness.

## Overpower swap (after Torment 12 + Dominate Glyph Lv. 100 + Banished Lord's Talisman)

Three swap directions documented. All require you to swap into Banished Lord's Talisman as the centerpiece charm and level Dominate to 100.

### Speedfarm Overpower

- Best for general play
- High damage output
- Incredible speed farmer AND boss farmer
- Easily farms **Pit 125**
- Kills last Mephisto phase in 2-3 seconds
- Easy to swap into
- Decently tanky

### Push Overpower

- Very tanky
- Highest damage output
- Incredible Tower pusher
- Tower 140+ with good gear
- **Tower 150+ with amazing Transfigured gear**
- Easy to swap into
- Requires proper cooldown management to maintain Fury

### Push Selig Overpower

- Exceptionally tanky
- Strong Tower pusher
- Tower 135+ with good gear
- Tower 145+ with amazing Transfigured gear
- Harder to swap into
- **Requires a whole new gearset with absolutely no life**
- Can face-tank Mephisto with **Endurant Faith** charm

(The Selig Overpower variant is the same family as [Rob Spin2Win](/builds/barbarian/whirlwind-speed/) and [swa99y Pelghain Frost](/builds/barbarian/whirlwind-pit150-frost/). They differ in talisman, ring choices, and CC strategy.)

## Patch 3.0.2 bug acknowledgment (from guide, verbatim)

> Crusher's 20% multiplier is not currently working with 2-Handed Maces.
> Aspect of Limitless Rage Does not gain stacks from Resource Generation sources.

Same bugs documented across the other Barb pages on this site. Use **Challenger** as the Crusher workaround. Don't temper "+X Fury per Second", use Lucky Hit Resource on jewelry instead.
