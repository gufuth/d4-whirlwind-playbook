:::tip[Big win]
**On the NMD tree, take Dread Revival + Gauntlet + the corrupted-roots-friendly nodes.** On Infernal Hordes, take Council Coffers (free chest with mythic seal chance) and skip "Infernal Horda" (the tempting double-vowel node, not worth the ether cost).
:::

:::tip[Do]
prime move

Level all 5 War Plans to rank 10. Unlocks hidden Mythic War Plan Quests. The gems quest reportedly drops one of each max-rolled Horadric jewel.
:::

### H1 — Tree of Whispers (two setups)

| Setup | Path | Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| **Farming** | LEFT → Corrupted Roots + Head Rotten Beast → RIGHT → Whisper Ambush + Colossal Foe + (optional) Varawn | Max corrupted-root + material yield while doing whispers |
| **Opening** | Middle → Wisdom of Whispers (+50% XP orbs) → Resplendent Favor (mythic sparks) → Corrupted Roots node | Max XP + sparks when handing in 10 grim favors |

:::caution[Caveat]
Respec is 50M gold/pass. Commit to one setup per session.
:::

### H2 — Nightmare Dungeon (mandatory nodes)

| Node | Effect |
|---|---|
| Gauntlet | Kills under shrine trap souls; respawn when shrine ends. Foundation of the Corrupted Roots farm. |
| Fearless Conviction | Conviction chest at dungeon end has high Tuned Primordial Dust chance. |
| Recurring Nightmare | Bosses can drop Escalation NMD sigils. Sustains the mythic loop. |
| Treasure Breaches + Goblin Fall + Horadric Hunters | Goblin land portals. Massive loot density. |
| Hated Foe + Meat Brothers (swap) | Butchers spawn in NMDs. More loot, more Profane Mine Cages. |
| Branching Pathways (Escalation only) | Choose reward type at final Asteroth tier. |
| **Dread Revival** | NMD bosses have a chance to revive after kill, more boss kills = more escalation sigils + mythic seals. Critical for the mythic seal loop. |

Optional fillers: Waking Spoils, Ambush of Cold (Beast of Ice), Dog of Asteroth (Asteroth loot in NMD).

### H3 — Hell Tide (one config fits all)

| Node | Effect |
|---|---|
| Wire and Wroth | Maggots spawn → XP globes + Pangs of Duriel |
| Tainted Shrines + Hated Foe + Meat Brothers | Butchers spawn in Hell Tides → drop Profane Mine Cages |
| **Profane Mine Cages (drink up to 3)** | +3 monster level → drastically boosted difficulty and rewards. Always run with at least 1 stack. |
| Night Terrors → Hellborn Replacement | Chains butchers into NMDs too |

### H4 — Undercity (priority order)

1. Gutted Filth (left), Portal Pranksters appear at beacons. With Mythic Tribute of Armament active, every prankster = guaranteed mythic. 4 mythics in one run is documented.
2. Idols of War (middle), extra tributes drop.
3. +1 Monster Power (top), last point.

:::danger[Don't]
trap

Skip the "extra chest but no useful contents" bait node.
:::

Pair with mythic-armament amalgamation (5 ancestral → 1 mythic) for constant stock. Main source of Resplendent Sparks.

### H5 — Lair Bosses (critical nodes)

| Node | Effect |
|---|---|
| Lair of Runes + Lair of Plenty | Both add +1 monster power → dramatically better boss loot quality and mythic rate |
| Ultimate Nemesis (3 nodes down-right) | Nemesis Portal after kill. **30-second window, enter fast.** Mythics drop. Can chain into deeper nemesis. |
| Gregori's Gambit | Synergy with Hell Tide chest spawns. High uniques chance. |
| Final 1–2 points | Customize per content (Winter Glass for sorc, Essu boots, etc.) |

### H6 — Infernal Hordes (now competitive)

:::tip[Do]
re-evaluated

Sayur's analysis: Bartuc (the Infernal Hordes lair boss) has high mythic seal drop rate, possibly higher than Mephisto. The ether requirement (666 to summon) is the bottleneck, but the upgrades below make it tractable. Now worth running, not skipping.
:::

| Node | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Bedlam** | Every chaos wave grants +50 ether. Cuts ether-grind in half. Enemies gain +1 monster power (acceptable trade-off). |
| **Total Chaos** | Chaos waves appear more frequently → more Bedlam procs → more ether/run. Trade: -1 infernal offering per wave. |
| **Method and Madness** | Infernal offerings more likely to share category with prior choice. Stabilizes stack-building for damage and ether stacking. |
| Black Pact + Devil's Deal | Extra ether + Infernal Challenges. Pairs with Seal of Riches for billions/run gold farming. |

To level the tree solo: party with 3+ players, rotate joins so all benefit from the XP-bonus end quest. Group XP also stacks with the §N18 trick.

### H7 — Pit (glyph leveling)

If glyphs are sub-150:

1. +1 Glyph Upgrade Chance (priority #1)
2. Butcher in Pit, when he dies, pit ends instantly + instant glyph upgrade
3. Heart of Stone, progression orbs from kills speed the pit
4. Thief Goblins, extra goblins drop progression orbs
5. Optional: Cronus Soul, XP boost (mediocre payoff)

### H8 — Mythic War Plan Quests (rank 10)

At rank 10 across all 5 trees, the reroll pool starts showing Mythic War Plan Quests. Each completed war plan gives 3 free re-rolls of the next plan's options. Reroll until you see what you want.

| Quest | Reward |
|---|---|
| Mythic Unique | 1 mythic unique on completion (called "underwhelming" by source) |
| Armor / Opus / XP / Mats | Themed bulk rewards |
| **Gems** (the white whale) | Reportedly drops one of each max-rolled Horadric jewel. Source has farmed thousands of war plans without seeing it yet. |

## Infernal Hordes refinements (Slivver, 2026)

:::caution[Black Pact double-pick bug]
On round 3+ when the Black Pact choice appears, click a normal option FIRST, then quickly click Black Pact before it disappears. You get BOTH. The combined description doesn't show all benefits but they stack. Often the difference between an ether-rich and ether-broke run.
:::

**Take the Council Coffers node.** Adds an extra 100-ether chest at run end with talisman drops including mythic seal chance. Best single Infernal Hordes node for loot value.

:::danger[Don't take "Infernal Horda"]
The tempting-looking node with the double-vowel name (Horda vs. Hordes) sounds like extra runs but is not worth the ether cost in practice per Slivver.
:::
