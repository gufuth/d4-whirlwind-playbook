title: 'M. Secret Portal'
---

A grey "echoring nightmare" portal skin (D3 throwback) from a hidden Trace of Echoes sequence (rare drop from Sideless Eye, party-shareable).

| Wave Range | Click This Shrine |
|---|---|
| 1–9 | Protection |
| 10–14 | Artillery |
| 15–19 | Lethal |
| 20 | Channeling |

Boss appears → kill it → achievement unlocks → portal skin. Missing a click by one wave is recoverable (fewer rewards, no fail).

:::caution[Caveat]
Bonus: complete wave 60 of Echoing Hatred for the "little crab with a knife" pet.
:::
