Top-tier Whirlwind Barb has become the most comfortable speed-farm build in the game. Consolidated from Rob's "Immortal Barbarian FHQ" (deep guide) and Rex's final season writeup. Hits 40+ trillion damage, AFK-viable on Uber Lilith at T12, completely unkillable on 99.99% of content.

### P1 — The core mechanic: Melted Heart of Selig

Melted Heart converts your *resource pool* into your damage buffer instead of life. Damage taken hits fury first with massive reduction. Counterintuitively, **the LESS max life you have, the MORE damage reduction Melted Heart applies.** This is why no-life builds become unkillable.

| Stat | Target | How |
|---|---|---|
| Max Life | **534 (baseline minimum)** | ZERO life rolls on any gear. Skip every life node on paragon EXCEPT the 2 required to unlock paths (one path-blocking life node + one fury-adjacent node). |
| Max Fury | 400+ (ideally 420+) | Tyrael's Might chest (resources + fury) + max-resource rolls on charms. Diminishing returns past 420. |
| Fury regeneration | 20+ minimum, 64+ for push | Masterwork on boots + pants + (optional) chest. Set bonus from Crucible (Berserking set) gives +10 fury/sec while berserking, multiplied by resource gen. |
| Endurance Faith charm | Mandatory | Spreads incoming damage over 4 seconds. The other half of the immortality equation. Banished Lord's Talisman swap is for damage push only (loses immortality). |

### P2 — Weapons: 2H Mace primary, expertise is what matters

| Slot | Weapon | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Assigned to Whirlwind | **2H Mace** (200 IP) | Mace gives fury-on-hit via expertise + 15× multiplicative damage while berserking + slow attack speed (each hit is a bigger packet). Assign via `S` then middle mouse button. |
| Technique slot | Polearm | Polearm expertise = lucky hit chance to restore primary resource. Even though you ATTACK with the mace, the technique slot applies its expertise to ALL attacks. Free resource sustain. |
| Off-hand 1H | Sword / axe / polearm (cosmetic) | Implicits no longer differ across weapon types post-LoH. Pick whatever transmog looks best. Doom Sword + Berserking trademark is a popular look. |
| Off-hand 1H #2 | Same logic | Weapon damage on off-hands DOES NOTHING — only the equipped (mace) weapon damage matters for whirlwind. Don't roll weapon damage on off-hands. |

:::caution[Caveat]
gem strength target

Top players run

240%+ Gem Strength

on the mace via mass-transfig (§B4). Doubles the per-gem multiplier. Combined with high-rank skulls (max-rank skulls > diamonds in same physical/all-damage bucket), this is the single biggest DPS lever.
:::

### P3 — Aspects, glyphs, paragon

| Slot | Aspect | Notes |
|---|---|---|

| Off-hand 1H | Crushing Aspect | 5% more multi than Heavy Hitting. Always-active because we're permanently fortified. |
| Off-hand 1H | Ramaladni's Magnum Opus (unique) | Bonus applies to all skills post-LoH. Multiplies whirlwind even with mace equipped. |
| 2H Slashing | Aspect of Channeling | Hold-to-channel whirlwind. Mandatory or you click-spam yourself into a wrist injury. |

| Gloves | Gohr's Devastating Grips (unique) | Massive whirlwind multi + pull-in CC. Non-negotiable. |
| Pants | Tibault's Will (unique) | Fury regen backbone. Mandatory until you find a better resource source. |

#### Glyph priority

1. Twister — by far the biggest damage glyph. ~1,000% dust devil damage at level 130. Place on Blood Rage board (115 strength).
2. Challenger — replaces Crusher (which is bugged on 2H Maces). Solid universal multiplier.
3. Wrath — fury on crit. Survivability + Limitless Rage stack generation.
4. Optional: Might, Exploit (auto-applies vulnerable — replaces spirit wolves uptime), Dominate (overpower variant only).

#### Paragon priority (in order)

1. Path to all Legendary Nodes through your boards. Don't fill rare/magic until done.
2. Glyph sockets unlocked.
3. Level Twister, then Challenger, then Wrath glyphs first. Twister to ~130 minimum.
4. Last board: Force of Nature (dust devil size, advanced) OR Weapons Master (ignore the "swap weapons" line; the +75% damage to healthy/injured is what you take it for, plus max-fury synergy with Might/Marshall glyph).

### P4 — Skill tree + runeword

Standard 5-skill setup: Whirlwind + 3 shouts (Rally, War Cry, Challenging Shout) + Wrath the Berserker + (optional decoration) Iron Skin. Some players drop Iron Skin entirely and run 5 skills — works fine.

:::danger[Don't]
bugged rune

Iron Skin Retaliation rune is bugged in 3.0.2 — does not trigger bleed and does not triple duration as written. Use Juggernaut rune instead (loses bleed scaling but is the functional option). Bleed-scaling paragon nodes are taken anyway for the strength + berserking-damage values.
:::

**Runeword:** Seir + Vex = +3 to all skills + 10% crit chance. The +3 boosts whirlwind, shouts, and ulti simultaneously. Crit chance helps hit 100% crit even without rolling it on every slot.

### P5 — Mercenary

Raheir is universal. See §A4. Bastion + Inspiration setup for general scaling. Iron Wolf's Call only if you actually scale through core skills (most don't on Whirlwind shape).

### P7 — Common questions

| Q | A |
|---|---|
| Do I need Tyrael's Might? | No. Any chest with max resource + fury generation + fury works. Tyrael's gives armor, movement speed, and DR — convenience, not requirement. |
| Diamonds vs Skulls in weapons? | Same additive bucket (physical + all damage). Pick whichever you have at higher rank. Endgame: all-skulls if you can max them; otherwise mix where the diamond is your highest-rank gem. |
| How do I get overpower stacks without active casting? | Wrath of the Berserker passive grants overpower on cast. Blood Boiling aspect grants one every 4 sec. Both auto-keep stacks if you're casting your ulti on cooldown. |
| Why is my damage only billions when the guide says trillions? | You're missing multiplier diversity (§A1), skill ranks (§A2), or paragon legendary nodes (§A3). Audit those before adjusting gear. |
