### J1 — Gift of the Tree (ends ~May 20)

:::tip[Do]
prime window

Every Whisper Cache drops an extra "Gift of the Tree" cache. Legendary whisper caches drop

two

gifts. Recursive multiplier, highest XP and material window of the season.
:::

#### 14-cache yield (measured)

| Reward | Amount |
|---|---|
| Gold | ~1 billion |
| Paragon | 1.5 levels (at paragon 270+) |
| Mythic Undercity Keys | 1 + enough legendary armament to make a 2nd |
| Sigil Powder | Stacks |

#### Prerequisites

- Practice Technique 3-set + Seal of Riches (up to 96% gold find, craft via 3-to-1 of cheap legendary shields)
- T10+ for the 300% gold multiplier
- Crafting Material Drop Rate rolls on every spare charm slot

#### Whisper tree — two configs

| Mode | Build |
|---|---|
| Farming | Single Cache Always Material Collection (always legendary cache, guaranteed 2 gifts each); Chaotic Favor; Whisper Ambush + Colossal Foe; (optional) Varawn |
| Opening | Wisdom of Whispers (+50% XP orbs, cap ~1.8M XP each); Resplendent Favor (mythic sparks); Corrupted Roots node |

:::caution[Caveat]
decay

After event ends, this farm degrades ~50%. Use the window.
:::

### J2 — Corrupted Roots NMD farm

Acquisition: corrupted roots drop from whisper caches (~1-in-4 of Collection of Chaos caches, less elsewhere). Gift of the Tree event roughly doubles supply.

:::danger[Don't]
strategy correction

The shrine affix you need only appears on war-plan-assigned NMDs, NOT on NMDs you open with your own sigils. You must teleport into the NMD via the war plan's mini-map button, then check the affix list (top-left of map screen). Shrine affixes are uncommon, expect to cycle through multiple war plans before finding one. After looting, don't kill the boss, reset the dungeon for a chance to re-roll the same affixes.
:::

#### Strategy

| # | Action |
|---|---|
| 1 | Open NMD. Do NOT use any shrines as you clear. |
| 2 | Clear the entire dungeon. Note all shrine locations (most have 3, some 4). |
| 3 | Do NOT kill the boss. |
| 4 | Teleport back to the first shrine. |
| 5 | Plant 5 corrupted roots, 1 at a time. Teleport out between each plant to skip the 30-sec cooldown. |
| 6 | Activate roots → activate shrine → kill everything. |
| 7 | Carry shrine buff (10–15 sec left) to shrine 2. Wait for buff expiration. Gauntlet souls respawn → kill. |
| 8 | Repeat at shrine 3. |
| 9 | Stay at shrine 3, Gauntlet souls respawn one more time = 4th wave. |
| 10 | End on Fearless Conviction chest with 150–265+ conviction stacks. |

#### Required setup

- Tree of Whispers: Corrupted Roots node
- NMD: Gauntlet, Fearless Conviction, Nemesis, Goblin Fall
- Bonus: Hell Tide → Hated Foe + Meat Brothers Replacement (respawnable butchers in NMD)
- Bonus: Lair Boss tree → Ambush of Cold + Dog of Asteroth (bonus bosses in NMD)

#### Per-run yield (~15 min)

Stacks of legendaries (several GA); charms, set charms, mythic seal chances; forgotten souls, primordial dust, crafting mats; ~1.5 paragon levels at high paragon; mythic tributes, cruxes, occasional mythic uniques; ~50% root recovery (sometimes 0, sometimes 8+).

#### Regional reward targeting — pick your NMD by region

Each region gives different drops from exposed-root bosses and their whisper caches. Pick the NMD whose region matches the resource you need.

| Region | Material drops | Equipment focus |
|---|---|---|
| **Skovos** (new expansion) | Tuning prisms + gem fragments | Weapons |
| **Hawezar** (SE main continent) | **Forgotten souls** + skull fragments | Gloves |
| **Nahantu** (expansion) | Scrolls of restoration | Jewelry (rings) |
| Kehjistan (south) | Gold + scattered prisms | Pants |
| Dry Steps (left, by Kbarwe) | Obducite | Boots |
| Scosglen (north) | Sigil powder | Helmets |
| Fractured Peaks | Ruby fragments | Chest armor (caches of helms) |

:::tip[Do]
target farm

Need forgotten souls (the universal crafting bottleneck)? Use roots in Hawezar NMDs. Need weapons for crafting bases? Skovos. Need rolling resin / scrolls? Nahantu. The Head Rotten Feast node makes boss caches always greater-tier.
:::

:::danger[Don't]
cons

Needs a strong, tanky, fast build (~8M combat toughness; <span class="d4-item" title="Alternative name for Tyrael's Sword of Justice. Mythic Unique 1H Sword. Elite kills reduce all skill cooldowns by 10s for 12s.">El'druin</span> for permaport recommended). Avoid Hunter dungeons. Never override shrines. Loot can despawn if too much drops. Party splits root density. Will not be worth running after Gift of the Tree ends.
:::
