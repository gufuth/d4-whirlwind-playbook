- [ ] **Diversify your damage multiplier types**, same-type adds, different-type multiplies Three crit damage multipliers stack additively (small box). Crit × Vulnerable × All Damage stack multiplicatively (big box). This is the single principle separating billions from trillions. See §A1. Why Your Damage Is Only Billions
- [ ] **Farm at Torment 10, not Torment 12 Drop rates between T10 and T12 are nearly identical. T10 lets you clear 2× faster**, more runs per hour, more total drops. The XP gap isn't enough to justify slower farming. (See §H, §I.) Sayur, Best Mythic Seal Method
- [ ] Turn on Advanced Tooltip Information Options → Gameplay. Without it you can't tell a max-roll affix from a trash-roll one. Biggest single QoL setting in the game. Try These Settings
- [ ] **Never use Entropic Tuning Prisms on weapons The top weapon outcome**, Gem Strength, up to 333%, only rolls on raw transfig. Entropic caps you below it permanently. Cube Mistakes · Don't Waste Resources · How to Lock GAs
- [ ] Lock greater affixes with the enchanter "no-change" trick before any cube work Occultist → Enchant → click the GA → No Change. Star icon disappears, GA is hard-locked against any reroll. Single most important crafting technique in the game. 4 sources
- [ ] **Use Kalian Tuning Prism on your amulet Amulets are the only slot that gets a second Legendary aspect. Item stays modifiable**, reroll until you land on a second aspect you want. 3 sources
- [ ] Level every War Plan to rank 10 (yes, Infernal Hordes too) Unlocks hidden Mythic War Plan Quests. The gems quest reportedly drops one of each max-rolled Horadric jewel. Updated Warplan Meta
